inTRODUCTION

Each warehouse has one or more inspectors associated with it. An inspector is a personnel responsible for verifying the cause of failure in a faulty part returned by the dealer.

 

As an Inspector, you must ensure that all parts assigned to you are thoroughly inspected and appropriate actions taken on them.

 

The Warranty Management application allows you to:

  • Inspect all parts assigned to you and take appropriate actions on them.

  • Notify the processor of any part rejected by you.

  • Query for claims, inventory items and parts.

The Warranty Management application thus allows you to efficiently manage parts inspection.
