SEARCH PARTS

The Warranty Management application allows you to search for replaced parts returned by any service provider, as well as replaced parts yet to be returned to a warehouse, by using the Search Parts option.

 

To go to the search page, click on the Part Returns tab on the left-hand pane. The tab will expand to show all folders under it. Click on the Define Search Query link. You can search for trucks based on various part related parameters on this page. You can also save a search for future viewing. To know more about creating or editing existing business conditions, click here.

 

Search Results

The results of your search will be shown in a tabular format. Click here to know more about the listing toolbar on the Search Results table. Click here to know more about navigating through the Search Results table. To view the part details on a single page, double click on that entry in the table.

 

The part detail page is divided into two sections, Part Details and History for ease of navigation.

  • Part Details: This section shows the following details: Part Serial Number, Part Number, Description, Quantity, Return Location, Due Date, Claim Number, Claim Fault Code, Claim Fault Found and Claim Caused By.

  • History: You can see the following details here:

  • Date and Time: Date and time of the action that was taken on the part.

  • User: Login of the user who took the last action on the part.

  • Status: Status of the part after this action was taken.

  • Carrier: Shipping carrier that was used to ship the part. This information will be shown only after a part is shipped by the dealer. If the part is being shipped from a warehouse to a supplier location, this field will show the carrier used by the part shipper for this shipment.

  • Shipment Date: The date of shipment for the part. This information will be shown only after the part is shipped by the dealer. If the part is being shipped from a warehouse to a supplier location, this field will show the date when the part was shipped to the supplier location.

  • Tracking Number: Tracking number of the shipment that was used to ship the part. This information will be shown only after the part is shipped by the dealer. If the part is being shipped from a warehouse to a supplier location, this field will show the tracking number associated with the shipment to the supplier location.

  • Bin Location: Location in the warehouse where the part was placed. This information is shown only after the part is received at the warehouse.

  • Inspection Result: Result of the inspection, as specified by the inspector. If this part is in a supplier location and the supplier has inspected this part, then the inspection result given by the supplier will be shown here.

  • Comments: Any comments by the user who performed the update.

Note: Only the most recent action, taken on the part, will be shown in the History section.

 

Edit Search

You can edit a search phrase and modify any of the search parameters.

 

To edit a search phrase,

  • Click on the button on the search result page.

  • The Edit Search Query page is shown. You can see the search conditions created by you here.

  • You can edit any parameter in this field.

  • To reset the data in this page, click Reset. All details entered by you will be reset to the default selections.

  • To execute the modified search conditions, click Execute. The search results will be shown in a tabular format.

You can also search the part using the PreDefined Search options listed under the Part Returns tab on the left-hand pane or click on the Predefined Search (Part Return) link under the Common Actions on the Home page. To search for a part, enter/select one or more parameters.

  • Claim Number: The number of the claim to which this part belongs.

  • Serial Number: Serial number of the machine or the part, depending on the claim type, on which you filed the claim.

  • Tracking Number: Tracking number of the part returned.

  • Status: Select the status of the part from the drop down list.

  • Return To Location: Location of the warehouse where the part is returned.

Once you set the search parameter, perform one of the following actions:

  • If you wish to save this search, then check the Save Search check-box. The search will be saved in the Search Folders section under the search phrase specified by you.

  • If you want to clear the options selected by you, click on the Reset button.

  • If you want to search for a part based on the condition you just created, click on the Search button. The page refreshes to show the search results in a tabular format.
