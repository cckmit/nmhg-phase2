MISCELLANEOUS PARTS

Apart from the parts available in the system, if you want to create a new part that should appear in the Miscellaneous part's drop-down list of the dealer while filing a claim, perform the following steps:

  • Click on Miscellaneous Parts tab under the Warranty Admin. This will reveal the folders Manage Miscellaneous Parts and Configure Miscellaneous Parts. Click on Manage Miscellaneous Parts tab.

  • Click on button in the listing toolbar.

  • Click on the  button to enter the following details:

  • Part Number: Enter the new part number in this field.

  • Description: Enter the description for the part against your local language.

  • Click Create.

Similarly, you can create multiple miscellaneous parts by click the button and entering the details for each new part you want to create. The new miscellaneous part you have created will appear on Manage Miscellaneous Parts page. Once the miscellaneous part has been created, you can update it too by clicking on the miscellaneous part entry appearing under Manage Miscellaneous Parts page. After editing the part number and description, click Update to save the updates.

 

Configure Miscellaneous Parts

Once the miscellaneous part has been created you are required to configure it so that it gets associated to a dealer and can appear in the drop-down list for the dealer while filing a claim.

 

To configure miscellaneous parts:

  • Click on Miscellaneous Parts tab under the Warranty Admin. This will reveal the folders Manage Miscellaneous Parts and Configure Miscellaneous Parts. Click on Configure Miscellaneous Parts tab.

  • Click on the button in the listing toolbar and enter the following details under Miscellaneous Parts Criteria section:

  • Configuration Name: Enter the name of the configuration.

  • Dealer: Select the dealer from the list. This miscellaneous part will appear in the drop-down list for this particular dealer.

  • Click on the button under Miscellaneous Parts Configuration section and enter the following details:

  • Part Number: Enter the part number you created under Manage Miscellaneous Parts.

  • Description: It auto populates.

  • Unit Price: Enter the unit price against the currency specific to the dealer.

  • UOM: Select the unit of measurement for the part.

  • Quantity: Enter the threshold quantity for the part.

  • Click Submit. You can see a new miscellaneous part configuration listed under Configure Miscellaneous Parts page.

Now, when a claim is filed, the dealer will get this new miscellaneous part in the drop down along with the Unit Price, UOM and Threshold quantity set for that part.
