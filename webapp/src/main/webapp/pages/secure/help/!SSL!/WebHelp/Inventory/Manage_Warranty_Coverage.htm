MANAGE WARRANTY COVERAGE

A retailed equipment has a set of warranties associated with it. A warranty can be standard warranty that comes along with the equipment, goodwill warranty that is provided by the original equipment manufacturers as goodwill or it can be extended warranty that is purchased by the end customer.

 

You can activate, terminate or modify the time period for a warranty coverage. To do this,

  • Click on Manage Warranty Coverage link under the Fleet Inventory Management tab listed under Inventory tab on the left pane. The Manage Warranty Coverage page lists the below three options:

  • Activate Coverages - To activate existing terminated coverages of the inventories.

  • Complete Termination - To terminate existing active coverages of the inventories.

  • Modify End Dates - To modify coverage end date of existing active coverages of the inventories.

  • Select any one of the above option to perform on the inventory(s).

  • Click on Select Additional Inventories link.

  • Enter the serial number of the inventory or first few digits of the serial number, click Search. Select the inventories and click Select. You will find that some data is pre-populated on this page. The data is derived from the serial number of the inventory.

Depending upon your selection, enter the required fields and click Continue>>. A new confirm window pops up with all the details. Click Submit to confirm the selection. A successful message is received confirming your action.
