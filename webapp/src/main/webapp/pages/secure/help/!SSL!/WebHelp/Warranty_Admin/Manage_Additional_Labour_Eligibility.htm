MANAGE ADDITIONAL LABOUR ELIGIBILITY

A service procedure is identified by the job-code associated with it. The job code allows easy identification of the labor performed on the equipment. In addition to the normal hours spent on the job, the dealer can specify any additional hours that were spent on the job. The claim amount will take these additional hours into account while calculating the cost of the claim. This utility is applicable to only those dealers that are added on the Additional Labor Eligibility page.

 

Perform the below steps to add a dealer to the Additional Labor Eligibility list:

  • Click on the Manage Additional Labour Eligibility tab under the Warranty Admin tab on the left-hand pane or click on the Manage Additional Labour Eligibility link under Common Actions on the Home page.

  • Click on the button on the Additional Labor Eligibility page to enter the name of the dealer to whom you want to give this utility. You can also select multiple dealers by clicking on the button and entering the dealer name. If you do not select any dealer on Manage Additional Labour Eligibility page, this utility will be applicable to all the dealers.

  • Click Save.
