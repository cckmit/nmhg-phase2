FIELD MODIFICATION

Field Modification is a type of claim that can be submitted when a service campaign has been performed on an equipment, by you. A service campaign is a service initiated by the manufacturer where a specific set of equipment in the field is marked to be serviced even without the failure. Such a service campaign is normally initiated based on early warning signals that are generated from the failure information gathered as part of warranty claims or service issues. A campaign is generally run to avoid serious potential damage that could occur if the marked set of equipment is not serviced. By doing so, the manufacturer can save the cost of additional damage that might occur due to that part.

 

Perform the following steps to create and assign field modification code for the service campaign:

  • Click on Field Modification tab under the Warranty Admin tab on the left-hand pane or click on the Field Modification link under Common Actions.

  • Click on the button in the listing toolbar and enter the following information:

  • Field Modification Code: Enter a unique code to identify this campaign.

  • Field Modification Class: Use the drop-down field to select the kind of campaign.

  • Start Date: Select a date from which this campaign will be conducted.

  • End Date: Select a date up to which this campaign will be conducted.

  • Field Modification Description: Specify a short description for this campaign.

  • Click on the link Click to download the serial number upload template to download the template to your desktop. In this template, enter the serial numbers of the equipments that will be affected by this service campaign. Save the file. Now click on Browse. Specify the path of the file on your desktop. Click Open on the pop-up window.

Actions

The following actions are available on Step 1 of 2 page:

  • Cancel: Click on this button to close this page without saving any data entered by you.

  • Save: Click on this button to save the data entered by you. It also allows you to view the list of affected serial numbers for this service campaign. A success message is shown and the View All Items link is shown when you click on this button.

  • Continue: Click on this button to save the data entered by you. This will take you to Step 2 of 2.

Step 2 of 2

This page is also divided into various sections. The fields under some sections on this page may be hidden. To view the fields, click on the section header. This will expand the section showing all fields under it. Likewise, clicking on the section header of an expanded section collapses it hiding all fields under it.

You can see the Campaign Information and Uploaded Serial Numbers sections displayed on top of this page. All data entered by you in these sections will be displayed. Click on View All Items link to open the View Affected Units page which lists all affected equipments as specified by you. The other sections displayed on this page are: Components to Replace, Service Procedures, Incidental Limits and Manage Documents.

 

Components to Replace

The Components Replaced section has two sub-sections: Parts Replaced Installed and Outside Parts/Services.

 

Parts Replaced Installed

In this sub-section you can specify details on all components that were replaced for fixing the failure.

To enter details for a replaced part, click on the option. A new row is added to this sub-section. In a row you need to mention the following information for each part:

  • Part Number: Enter the part number of the part that must be replaced as per this campaign.

  • Quantity: Enter the number of parts of the given specification that must be replaced from the equipment.

  • Description: This is a read-only field and shows a short description of the part to be replaced.

  • Return Location: Specify the warehouse location where the replaced part must be returned by the service provider.

  • Payment Description: Select the payment description from the drop-down.

  • Due Days: Enter the number of days within which the replaced part must be returned to the specified location.

  • Shipped by OEM: Select this check-box if the installed part was shipped by the service provider.

You can add multiple values in this sub-section by clicking the option. You can delete any row by clicking the option corresponding to that row.

 

Outside Parts/Services

In this sub-section you can claim expenses related to any outside services or parts that the dealer used for fixing the failure. To enter details for a part/service, click on the option. A new row is added to this sub-section. In a row you need to mention the following information for each part/service:

  • Description: Enter a short description for the outside part or service that needs to be used for this campaign.

  • Quantity: Enter the number of parts of the given specification that must be installed.

  • Unit Price Limit: Enter the cost limit that can be incurred for the specified part/service.

You can add multiple values in this sub-section by clicking the option. You can delete any row by clicking the option corresponding to that row.

 

Miscellaneous Parts

Apart from the parts available in the system, if you want to enter any new parts information, you can enter it under Miscellaneous Parts section. To know more about Miscellaneous Parts, click here.

  • Part Number: Enter the new part number.

  • Description: It auto-populates.

  • Quantity: Enter the quantity for the part.

  • Unit Price: It auto-populates.

  • UOM: The unit of measurement for the part will auto-populate. Click here, for more information.

Service Procedures

The Service Procedures section captures the service procedures to be performed in order to administer this campaign.

  • Click on the option.

  • The Select Service Procedures pop-up is shown. You can see a tree like structure of service procedures here.

  • Click on the icon in front of a service procedure to view the entire structure.

  • Check the check-box against the service procedure you want to select.

  • You can select multiple service procedures on this window.

  • Now click on the Add Service Procedure button. All service procedures selected by you will be shown as different rows under the Service Details section.

You can view/enter the following details for each service procedure:

  • Job Code: The service procedure selected by you. It will also show a short description of the service procedure.

  • Use Labor Standards: Check the check-box for standard labor hours.

  • Specified Labor Hours: Time, in hours and minutes, required to perform the labor.

You can delete a service procedure by clicking the option corresponding to that row.

 

Incidental Limits

You can specify the maximum allowable limits for the incidentals such as Travel Distance, Location, Trips, Hours, Parking and Toll expense, any Rental Charges etc. in this section.

 

Supplier Recovery Info

For a supplier recovery claim, enter the supplier recovery information. Thus, when the claim is submitted and accepted by the processor and is sent for supplier recovery as supplier recovery claim, these contract details will be displayed for that claim.

  • Contract: Select the name of the contract from the available list of contracts present in the system.

  • Contract Name: It auto-populates. To view more details regarding the contract, click on the link on the contract name.

  • Supplier: It auto-populates.

  • Supplier Number: It auto-populates.

Manage Documents

In this section you can attach binary documents, providing additional details about this campaign, for the service provider's reference. To attach a document, click on the option. A pop-up window is shown. Click on the Browse button to browse through your system for the document. After you have mentioned the exact location of the document, click Attach. All selected documents will be attached to the campaign. To delete an entry, click on the option.

 

Actions

The following actions are allowed on a campaign:

  • Previous: Click on this button to go back to Page 1 of 2. You can make changes to any field on this page.

  • Save: Click on this button to save the data entered by you.

  • Click on Assign Field Modification For All Items to assign the campaign to all affected units.

Once you have assigned a field modification for an item, again click on Field Modification tab under the Warranty Admin tab on the left-hand pane or click on the Field Modification link under Common Actions. Select the field modification code assigned by you, double click on the entry and click Activate Field Modification button.

 

A notification of the same is listed in your Pending Field Modifications folder. To know more about pending field modifications, click here. You can now file a claim for all the items on which service campaign has been performed. For further information about creation and submission of filed modification claim, click here.

 

You can also edit the field modification information entered by you.

To edit the Field Modification information,

  • click on Field Modification tab under the Warranty Admin tab on the left-hand pane or click on the Field Modification link under Common Actions.

  • Select and double click on the assigned field modification entry you want to edit.

  • Modify the entries to be edited. You can also add/remove the inventories from field modification. If you want to add items to the field modification, select the radio button, Add Items. Select the radio button Remove Items to remove the inventories from field modification.

  • Click on the link Click to download the serial number upload template to download the template to your desktop. In this template, add/remove the serial numbers of the equipments that are affected by the service campaign. Save the file. Now click on Browse. Specify the path of the file on your desktop. Click Open on the pop-up window.

Actions

The following actions are available on Step 1 of 2 page:

  • Save: Click on this button to save the data entered by you. It also allows you to view the list of affected serial numbers for this service campaign. A success message is shown and the View All Items link is shown when you click on this button.

  • Continue: Click on this button to save the data entered by you. This will take you to Step 2 of 2.

You can add/edit the information entered on page 2 of 2 and perform any one of the below actions:

  • Previous: Click on this button to go back to Page 1 of 2. You can make changes to any field on this page.

  • Save: Click on this button to save the data entered by you.

  • Deactivate Field Modification: Click on this button to deactivate this field modification generated.

Depending upon the selection of Add Items or Remove Items on Page 1 of 1, one of the following options will appear.

  • Remove Items from Field Modification: Click on this button to remove the selected items from the field modification.

  • Assign Field Modification For All Items: Click on this button to assign the campaign to all affected units.
