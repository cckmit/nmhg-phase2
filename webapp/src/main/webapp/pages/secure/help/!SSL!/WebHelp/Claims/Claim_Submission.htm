claim SUBMISSION

Sometimes, a service provider is unable to submit due to inaccessibility of the Warranty Management system. In such cases, you can collect the necessary details from the service provider and submit the claim on behalf of him/her.

 

To submit a claim,

  • Click on the Create Claim link under the Go To section of your Home page.

  • Click on the button on the listing toolbar of the Draft, Forwarded, Service Manager Response, Pending Field Modifications, Stock or Retail folders.

Any of the above actions bring up the New Claim page, also referred to as the claim submission page.

Note: The Warranty Management application allows you to create several claims simultaneously. Each time you click on the Create Claim option, a New Claim page appears, which allows you to enter details for a new claim.

Claim creation is a two step process. As part of the first step, you provide some very basic information about the product for which the claim is being made. After verifying some preliminary data related to the product, you are taken to the next step to complete the remaining part of the claim. The claims would capture different details some as part of step 1 and mostly as part of step 2 depending on the claim type and product.

 

You can submit the following types of claims using the Create Claim option:

  • Machine Claim

  • Parts Claim

  • Field Modification Claim

To view instructions for submitting the above types of claim, click on the links above.
