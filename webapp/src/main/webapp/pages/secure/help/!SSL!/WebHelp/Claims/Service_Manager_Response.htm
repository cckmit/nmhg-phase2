Service Manager Response

While submitting a claim, you have an option to send the claim to the District Service Manager (DSM) for review and approval. The DSM is associated to the dealership to which the dealer, on whose behalf you are submitting the claim, belongs to. These claims will either be approved or rejected by the DSM, and sent back to you. All replies sent by the DSM will be stored in the Service Manager Response folder. The total number of claims in this folder will be indicated by the number in the parenthesis following the folder.

 

To view all service manager responses, click on the Claims tab on the left-hand pane. The tab will open to reveal the folders under it. Click on the Service Manager Response folder. On this page you can see the DSM Response table that lists all responses sent by the DSM. For further information on navigating through the table, see Lists.

 

The DSM Response table provides the following information about each draft claim:

  • Claim Number: Serial number of the claim.

  • Claim Type: Kind of claim. The claim can be of type machine, parts or campaign.

  • Dealer: Name of your dealership.

  • Fault Code: Failure code depending on the failure that has occurred. This field will be blank for a campaign claim.

  • Causal Part: Part serial number or part number of the causal part.

  • Date of Failure: Date on which the equipment/part failed. This field will be blank for a campaign claim.

  • Date of Repair: Date when the repair work was performed.

To view the claim details on a single page, double click on the claim number of that claim. The Claim No. (n) page is shown. All information relevant to the selected claim will be displayed under various sections. You cannot edit any details on this claim.

 

Actions

The following actions are allowed on a claim in the Service Manager Response folder:

  • Attach documents: You can attach binary documents, provided by the dealer, in the Supporting Documents section. To attach a document, click on the option. A new row is shown under this section. Click on the Browse button to navigate through your system for the document. Click on Attach to attach the document to your claim.

  • Comments: Enter any comments you may have about the claim here.

  • Cancel: Click on this button to close this page without saving any details entered by you.

  • Re-request for SMR: This button is shown only if your claim was denied by the DSM. Click on this button to resend your claim for a SMR. The number of times you can resend a claim for SMR is a pre-configured parameter.

  • Submit: This button is shown only if the claim was accepted by the DSM. Click on this button to send your claim to the OEM for recovery.

  • Discard: Click on this button to delete this claim.

Depending on the action taken by you, a success message will be shown confirming your action. The message will hide itself and the current page will be closed.
