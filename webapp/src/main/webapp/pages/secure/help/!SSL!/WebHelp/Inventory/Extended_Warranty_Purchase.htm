EXTENDED warranty PURCHASE

A retailed equipment has a set of warranties associated with it. A warranty can be standard warranty that comes along with the equipment, goodwill warranty that is provided by the original equipment manufacturers as goodwill or it can be extended warranty that is purchased by the end customer. All standard warranties have an end date and hence the end customer has the option to purchase any extended policies against a retailed Inventory.

 

You can purchase the extended warranty in the following ways:

  • Click on Retailed tab under Inventory on left-hand pane. Choose the inventory item and click on   button in the listing toolbar.

  • Go to the Home page, click on the Inventory link under Quick Search. Enter the serial number of the inventory for which you want to extend the warranty and select Extended Warranty Purchase.

In addition, you can also select multiple inventories for extended warranty by performing the following steps:

  • Click on Select Additional Inventories link on the right-hand side of the page.

  • Enter the serial number of the inventory or first few digits of the serial number, click Search. You can also enter other details like Model number and End Customer information to search the inventories. A list of inventories along with their details will appear.

  • Select the inventories on which you want to extend the warranty purchase.

  • Click Select.

Once the inventories have been selected for extended warranty purchase, select the checkbox against those selected inventories and enter the following details:

  • Date of Purchase: Enter the date of purchasing the warranty. On entering the date of purchase you will see a list of warranty plans appearing against the inventory you selected above. Select the warranty plan(s) for those inventories. You can also attach any supporting document by clicking on the Browse button.  

  • Purchase Order Number: Enter the purchase order number.

  • Comments: Enter the comments in this field.

Once you have entered all the details, accept the terms and conditions by selecting the checkbox and click Continue >>. A new window pops-up to confirm the extended warranty purchase details entered by you. Click Purchase. A success message will be shown confirming your action.

 

Window Period for Extended Warranty Purchase

There is a specific time frame within which you can purchase an extended warranty policy. This specific time frame is called window period.

 

To view/edit the window period for extended warranty policy,

  • Click on Manage Warranty Policies tab under the Warranty Admin tab on the left-hand pane. This will reveal the folder Policy Definition. Click on Policy Definition.

  • Click on the policy for which you want to view/edit the window period. There are three fields that define the window period for extended warranty policy purchase.

  • Extended Warranty Purchase Start Window Period From Delivery Date: View/Enter the number of months that will be added to the delivery date to calculate the start window period date for purchasing the extended warranty.

  • Extended Warranty Purchase End Window Period from Delivery Date: View/Enter the number of months that will be added to the delivery date to calculate the end window period date for purchasing the extended warranty.

  • Extended Warranty Purchase End Window Period from Shipment Date: View/Enter the number of months that will be added to the shipment date to calculate the end window period date for purchasing the extended warranty.

Both the Extended Warranty Purchase End Window Period from Delivery Date and Extended Warranty Purchase End Window Period from Shipment Date calculate the end date for purchasing the extended warranty, however, the system picks the date whichever is less.
