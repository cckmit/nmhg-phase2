Claim ATTRIBUTES

The Warranty Management application allows you to add attributes to the claim submitted by any service provider.

 

To add claim attributes,

  • Click on the Claim Attributes tab under the Multi Claim Maintenance folder under the Claims tab on the left-hand pane. You can search for claims based on various claim parameters on this page.

You can search for positive results for a set of conditions that you define on this page, or you can also search for a negation of the set of conditions defined on this page. To know more about creating or editing existing business conditions, click here.

 

The page refreshes to show the search results in a tabular format.

  • Select the checkbox for the claims for which you want the attribute to be associated.

  • Under the Attribute section, select the attributes. There are different types of attributes: Processing Note, Review Responsibility, Rejection Reason, Acceptance Reason and Technician. You can select one or more attributes.

  • Click Submit.

Depending upon your selection, a particular text box will appear when the claim is in the processor inbox. The processor can enter the comments in that text box.
