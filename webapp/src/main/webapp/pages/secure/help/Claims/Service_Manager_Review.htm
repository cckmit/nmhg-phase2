Service Manager ReVIEW

A dealer belonging to your dealership is allowed to forward claims to you, for review, before submitting it to the OEM for reimbursement. All claims sent by the dealers will appear in your Service Manager Review folder. You can review the claim and then approve or deny it.

 

To view all claims sent to you for review, click on the Claims tab on the left-hand pane. The tab will open to reveal the folders under it. Click on the Service Manager Review folder. On this page you can see the SMR Claims table that lists all claims saved as draft.

 

The SMR Claims table provides the following information about each draft claim:

  • Claim Number: Serial number of the claim.

  • Claim Type: Kind of claim. The claim can be of type machine, parts or campaign.

  • Dealer: Name of the filing dealer's dealership.

  • Fault Code: Failure code depending on the failure that has occurred. This field will be blank for a campaign claim.

  • Causal Part: Part serial number or part number of the causal part.

  • Date of Failure: Date on which the equipment/part failed. This field will be blank for a campaign claim.

  • Date of Repair: Date when the repair work was performed.

To view the claim details on a single page, double click on the claim number of that claim. The claim will be shown in the detailed page, Claim No. (n). All information relevant to the selected claim will be displayed under various sections for ease of navigation. For further information on the claim detail page, click here.

 

On the claim detail page, you can make any changes to the claim sent by the dealer, except for the Claim Header and the Equipment Details sections.

 

Actions

You can perform the following actions on the claims in your Service Manager Review folder:

  • Internal Processing Notes: Enter any comments you may have about this claim, that will be useful for other OEM personnel verifying this claim. The comments entered in this field can be seen by OEM personnel only.

  • Comments to Dealer: Enter any comments you may have for the dealer.

  • Cancel: Click on this button if you want to close the claim detail page without saving any data entered by you.

  • Validate: Click on this button to validate the claim. Click here for more details on claim validation.
