Retailed Inventory

The Retailed folder contains detailed information on your merchandised inventory. The data in this folder allows you to efficiently search for an equipment that you have sold to an end customer.

 

To view your retailed inventories,

  • Click on the Inventory tab on the left-hand pane. This reveals the folders under this tab, Stock and Retailed. Click on the Retailed folder.

  • Use the Inventory Query option to query for all inventories retailed by you. Click here to know more about inventory querying.

On the right-hand pane, you can see the Retailed Inventory table that lists your retailed inventory. For further information on the navigating through the table, see Lists.

 

The Retailed Inventory table provides the following information about each equipment:

  • Serial Number: The serial number of the equipment. This number is a unique identifier assigned to this equipment.

  • Item Number: This is the number of the item to which this equipment belongs. This number determines the unique class of product of which type the equipment is.

  • Model: The model number of the product to which this equipment belongs.

  • Product: The product to which the inventory item belongs.

  • Warranty Start Date: Date when this equipment will be covered by the warranty policies associated with it. This data will not be displayed for an equipment in your stock inventory.

  • Warranty End Date: Date when this equipment will be out of warranty, i.e. the policies covering this equipment will be inactivated. This data will not be shown for an equipment in your stock inventory.

By default, details of the first equipment in Retailed Inventory table will be displayed in the preview pane in a tabbed view.

 

Equipment Details

Click on an equipment to view its details in the preview pane. To view the equipment details on a single page, double click on the equipment in the Retailed Inventory table. All details of the selected equipment will be shown on the equipment details page of that equipment.

 

You can view the following data for the selected equipment on in the preview pane and detail page: equipment info, details of all transaction associated with this equipment, a list of all warranty policies registered on this equipment, a history of claims filed on this equipment, and a list of pending service campaigns on this equipment. Click here to know more about the equipment details page.

 

Actions

The following actions are allowed on the equipments in your Retailed folder:

  • Equipment Transfer: Clicking on this button brings up the Equipment Transfer page for the selected equipment. Click here to know more about equipment transfer.

  • Extended Warranty Purchase: Clicking on this button brings up the Extended Warranty Purchase page for the selected equipment. Click here to know more about extended warranty purchase.

  • Create Claim: Clicking on this button brings up the claim submission page for this equipment. Click here to know more about submitting claims.

  • Submit DCAP Claim: Clicking on this button brings up the DCAP caim submission page. Click here to know more about DCAP claim.

In addition to above actions, you can perform the below action when you click on the retailed inventory listed on the Retailed Inventory table.

  • Reports: Clicking on this page will open the Custom Reports page. You can fill these reports and submit it for this equipment.
