RETAIL MACHINE Transfer

Retail Machine transfer (RMT) is the process of transferring retail inventories from one dealership to another dealership.

 

To file a RMT,

  • Click on the Inventory tab on the left-hand pane. This reveals the folders under this tab, Stock, Retailed, Fleet Inventory Management. Click on the Fleet Inventory Management folder. This folder will reveal the sub folder Retail Machine Transfer. Click on Retail Machine Transfer.

  • Click on Select Additional Inventories link on the right-hand side of the page.

  • Enter the serial number of the equipment or first few digits of the serial number, click Search. If you want to file RMT on multiple inventories, select the inventories and click Select. You will find that some data is pre-populated on this page. The data is derived from the serial number of the equipment on which you are generating the RMT.

  • Click on Perform Transfer to input the new dealer details.

  • Enter the Dealer Name or Dealer Number to search dealer.

  • Click Select Dealer. A RMT details page will open reflecting information for the inventory selected for RMT and also the details for both the previous dealer and the new dealer selected.

  • Click Confirm.

You can also view a new transaction added under the Transaction History section. This describes the transfer of the dealership of the equipment from the current owner to the new dealer. For further details on the equipment details page, click here.
