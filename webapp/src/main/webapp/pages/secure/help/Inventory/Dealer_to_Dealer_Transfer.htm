DEALER TO DEALER TRANSFER

Dealer to Dealer Transfer is transferring the stocks from one dealer to other dealer.

 

To perform Dealer to Dealer Transfer,

  • Click on Stock tab under Inventory on left-hand pane. Choose the inventory item and click on button in the listing toolbar or

  • Click on Dealer to Dealer Transfer under the Fleet Inventory Management folder present under the Inventory tab on the left had pane or

  • Go to the Home page, click on the Inventory link under Quick Search. Enter the serial number of the inventory you want to transfer and select Dealer to Dealer to continue.

To select multiple inventories for transfer:

  • Click on Select Additional Inventories link on the right-hand side of the page.

  • Enter the serial number of the inventory or first few digits of the serial number, click Search. A list of inventories along with their details will appear.

  • Select the inventories which you want to transfer and click Select.

  • Click Perform Transfer. A new page will pop-up with the list of all the selected inventories marked for transfer.

  • Enter the Dealer Name or Dealer Number. Click Search Dealer.

  • Select the dealer form the list of searched dealers.

  • Click Select Dealer. A new page with a list all the selected inventories marked for transfer along with the details of previous dealer and the new dealer will appear.

  • Click Confirm.

A success message will be shown confirming your action. All the selected stocks will be transferred under the new dealer.
