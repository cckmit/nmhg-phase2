SCRAP/UNSCRAP InventorIES

The Warranty Management application allows you to scrap inventories which are completely non functional and are of no use. At the same time scrapped inventories can also be unscrapped.

 

Perform the following steps to mark the inventory as scrap:

  • Go to the Home page, click on the Inventory link under Quick Search. Enter the serial number of the inventory you want to mark as scrap and click Continue>> or

  • Click on Scrap Inventories under the Fleet Inventory Management folder present under the Inventory tab on the left had pane or

  • Click on Stock under the Inventory tab on the left-hand pane. Select the inventory from the Stock Inventory table and click on  button in the listing toolbar. A new scrap inventories detail page will appear.

You can also select multiple inventories to mark as scrap. Perform the following steps to mark multiple inventories as scrap:

  • Click on Select Additional Inventories link on the right-hand side of the page.

  • Enter the serial number of the inventory or first few digits of the serial number, click Search. You can also enter other details like Dealer Number, Dealer Name, Model number, Factory Order Number or End Customer information to search the inventories. A list of inventories along with their details will appear.

  • Select the inventories which you want to mark as scrap.

  • Click Select.

  • Date of Scrap: Enter the date on which you want to mark the inventory as scrap.

  • Comments: Enter the comments in this field.

  • Click Submit. A new window pops-up to confirm the scrap. Click ‘Yes’ to confirm otherwise click ‘No’. A success message will be shown confirming your action. If you click Cancel, it will disregard the action and will move you to the previous page.

You can reuse the inventory that was earlier marked as scrap by remarking it as unscrap. The inventory will reappear under your stock list.

 

Perform the following steps to mark the inventory as unscrap:

  • Go to the Home page, click on the Inventory link under Quick Search. Enter the serial number of the inventory you want to mark as unscrap and click Continue>> or

  • Click on Un-Scrap Inventories under the Fleet Inventory Management folder present under the Inventory tab on the left had pane or

  • Click on Stock under the Inventory tab on the left-hand pane. Select the inventory from the Stock Inventory table and click on button in the listing toolbar. A new unscrap inventories detail page will appear.

You can also select multiple inventories to mark as unscrap. Perform the following steps to mark multiple inventories as unscrap:

  • Click on Select Additional Inventories link on the right-hand side of the page.

  • Enter the serial number of the inventory or first few digits of the serial number, click Search. You can also enter other details like Dealer Number, Dealer Name, Model number, Factory Order Number or End Customer information to search the inventories. A list of inventories along with their details will appear.

  • Select the inventories which you want to mark as unscrap.

  • Click Select.

  • Date of Unscrap: Enter the date on which you want to mark the inventory as unscrap.

  • Comments: Enter the comments in this field.

  • Click Submit. A new window pops-up to confirm the scrap. Click ‘Yes’ to confirm otherwise click ‘No’. A success message will be shown confirming your action. If you click Cancel, it will disregard the action and will move you to the previous page.

Note:

  • When retail inventory is scrapped with the consent of an end customer, all the active policy coverage present on it gets terminated. However if marked unscrap, all the terminated and suspended policy gets activated and is retained once again.

  • You cannot file a Warranty claim or Field Modification claim and also cannot perform Warranty Registration, Equipment Transfer, Retail Machine Transfer and Dealer to Dealer Transfer on an inventory if it is mark as scrapped.
