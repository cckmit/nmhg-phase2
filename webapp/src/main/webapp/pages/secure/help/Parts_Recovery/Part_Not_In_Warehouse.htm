PART NOT IN WAREHOUSE

The part shipper collects all parts marked for supply recovery from the bins in the warehouse and ships them to the location specified by the Supply Recovery Admin (SRA). However, if the return location is not specified, the claim will appear in the Part Not In Warehouse folder.

 

To view the parts that do not have return location specified, click on the Parts Recovery tab on the left-hand pane. The tab will open to reveal the folders under it. Click on the Part Not In Warehouse folder.

 

The Part Not In Warehouse table provides the following information:

  • Recovery Claim Number: The identification number of the claim to which the part to be returned belongs.

  • Dealer: Name of dealer who sent this shipment.

  • Product: The product name of the equipment to which this part belongs.

  • Model: Model number of the equipment to which this part belongs.

  • Causal Part: The causal part number of the part being shipped for recovery.

  • Fault Location: Failure location as entered by the dealer.

  • Status: The current status of the claim.

Double clicking on any claim will open a new page. Under the Part Details section, enter the return location and number of days within which the part should be received.

  • Location: Name of the location to which you are making the shipment.

  • Due Days: Number of days between the current date and the due date for this part. If the part is not received within the specified days, then the claim will move under the Part Shipped Not Received inbox.

Click Submit to save the details. The claim will now move under the Awaiting Shipment to Warehouse folder.
