DOWNLOAD MANAGEMENT

The Download feature allows you to download the desired type of reports on your local machine. You need to select the desired download type from the available list such as Warranty Claim Detail Data and click Submit. The download page for that particular selection appears.

 

Perform the following steps for Download Management:

  • Click on Manage Uploads/Downloads tab under the Reports tab on the left-hand pane.

  • Click Download Management. The Download Management page appears. Select the desired download type from the available list such as Warranty Claim Detail Data and click Submit.

  • The download page for that particular selection appears.

  • Enter the required details and click Submit

  • The File Download dialog box appears from which you can open or save the file to your system.
