InTRODUCTION

When a service provider files a claim, the part causing the failure in the truck is returned to the warehouse. This part may be sourced from a supplier and may be covered under a supplier contract. All such parts need to be returned to the supplier for recovery and reimbursement, as stated in the contract covering it. A Supplier Recovery Processor (SRP) reviews the recovery claim submitted by a SRA and decide the payment that must be done to the supplier.

 

As a SRP, you can edit any details in the claim, accept or reject the claim, put the claim on hold or transfer it to another processor.

 

The Warranty Management application allows you to:

  • Negotiate with supplier to arrive at final recovery payment.

  • Transfer a supplier claim to another processor.
