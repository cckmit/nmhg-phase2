INVENTORY

Inventory refers to a collection of trucks. All trucks at hand are termed as Stock Inventory and all trucks sold to end users are termed as Retailed Inventory. As an Inspector, you can view detailed truck information, transaction history of each truck and a list of all claims that were filed on the truck.

 

In the left-hand pane, click on the Inventory tab. The tab opens to reveal the folders under it:

  • Stock

  • Retailed

To access any of these folders, you must click on that folder link. A new page, displaying the list of inventories, will appear in the right-hand pane. Double-click on the inventory to view the details.
