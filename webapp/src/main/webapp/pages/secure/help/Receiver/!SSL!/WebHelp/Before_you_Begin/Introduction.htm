INTRODUCTION

When a dealer replaces a part, sometimes he/she is asked to return the replaced part back to the OEM for inspection or recovery from the supplier. After the dealer physically ships a part, he/she enters all relevant shipment details into the Warranty Management application. These details will appear in the receiver's inbox. As a receiver, you must ensure that all parts marked to be returned to your warehouse are received through the shipment specified by the service provider.

 

When the parts have been shipped by the service provider, they are to be received by the parts receiver assigned to the return location to which the parts are shipped.

 

As a receiver, the Warranty Management application allows you to:

  • Maintain records of parts received at the warehouse.

  • Mark missing parts in shipments as not returned, thus alerting the service provider to return the part.

The Warranty Management application thus allows you to maintain all details of replaced parts submitted by dealers to the warehouse, in an efficient manner.
