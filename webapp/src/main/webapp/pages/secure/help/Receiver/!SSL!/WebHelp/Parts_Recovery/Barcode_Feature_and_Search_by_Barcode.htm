BARCODE FEATURE AND SEARCH BY BARCODE

Some businesses require each part that is being returned to bear a barcode. The barcode is provided to the dealers by the Business. This barcode then becomes a unique identifier for the part and it helps the business to track various other workflows on it such as vendor recovery and part inspection. The barcode is a unique number that is maintained by the business and sent out to the dealerships. The dealers are expected to attach a barcode with each part that is being returned.

 

The barcode is a 7 digit number that is validated using an algorithm. If the barcode feature is used, then the system lets the user specify the shipment status at each part level (whether being shipped or not). Once the shipment is generated by the dealer, the system now moves the record to the Shipment Generated inbox.

 

Search by Barcode

This feature enables you to search for a claim using its barcode number.

 

PreDefined Search

To search for a claim,

  • Click on PreDefined Search link under the Search folders tab under the Part Returns tab on the left-hand pane.

  • The Part Return Search section is displayed. It contains various search criteria to search for the desired claim.

  • To search for a claim using its barcode number, enter the barcode number in the Barcode number field and click Search.

  • This displays the details of the claim such as Part Number, Return Location and other fields for the entered barcode number.

  • Double click on the entry to display the complete claim details for that claim.
