AWAITING SHIPMENT TO WAREHOUSE

Once the return location for shipment is mentioned under the Part Not In Warehouse folder, the claim moves under the Awaiting Shipment to Warehouse folder.

 

To view the parts to which the return location has been mentioned and are awaiting shipment, click on the Parts Recovery tab on the left-hand pane. The tab will open to reveal the folders under it. Click on the Awaiting Shipment to Warehouse folder.

 

The Awaiting Shipment to Warehouse table provides the following information:

  • Recovery Claim Number: The identification number of the claim to which the part to be returned belongs.

  • Dealer: Name of dealer who sent this shipment.

  • Product: The product name of the equipment to which this part belongs.

  • Model: Model number of the equipment to which this part belongs.

  • Fault Location: Failure location as entered by the dealer.

  • Status: The current status of the claim.

Double clicking on any claim will open a new page, Recovery Claim Number <n>. You can view the return location and the due days for returning the part under the Part Details section.
