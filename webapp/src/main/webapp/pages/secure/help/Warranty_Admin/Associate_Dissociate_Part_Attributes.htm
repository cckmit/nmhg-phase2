ASSOCIATE/DISSOCIATE PART ATTRIBUTES

Once an attribute has been created, you can associate it to any part. Perform the following steps to associate an attribute to a part.

  • Click on Additional Attributes tab under the Warranty Admin tab on the left-hand pane. Click on Associate/Dissociate Part Attributes tab under Additional Attributes. A Manage Attributes page will open on the right-hand side.

  • Click on the attribute you want to associate. You can view the attribute details that were selected while creating the attribute.

  • Click on button under the Associate Parts section.

  • Enter the Part Number to which you want this attribute to be associated. You can also associate an attribute to multiple parts by clicking on button. To delete a row, click on the option corresponding to that row.

  • Click Submit. You will receive a success message confirming your action. If you click Cancel, it will disregard the action and move you to the previous page.

You can also dissociate an attribute by clicking on the option corresponding to the product/inventory with which you want to dissociate the attribute. Once you click Submit, the attribute will no longer be associated to that part.

 

Once you have associated an attribute to a part and submitted a claim, you will see Enter Additional Attribute link under Failure Information section on page 2 of 2 of the New Claim page. This link will appear when you enter the Casual Part Number to which you associated the attribute. You can add any additional information here which may be text, date or number depending upon the data type selected while creating the attribute.

Note: Only the processor has the ability to change the attribute while the claim is still in the processor inbox. Once the claim is accepted, the attribute cannot be changed.
