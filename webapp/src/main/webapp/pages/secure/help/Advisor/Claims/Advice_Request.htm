ADVICE REQUEST

When a processor or an admin is reviewing a claim submitted by a dealer, they can send the claim to you requesting your advice. All claims sent to you for advice, will be shown in your Advice Request folder.

 

To view all claims sent to you for advice, click on the Claims tab on the left-hand pane. The tab will open to reveal the folders under it. Click on the Advice Request folder. On this page you can see the Advice Request table that lists all claims saved as draft. For further information on navigating through the table, see Lists.

 

The Advice Request table provides the following information about each draft claim:

  • Claim Number: Serial number of the claim.

  • Claim Type: Kind of claim. The claim can be of type machine, parts or campaign.

  • Dealer: Name of the filing dealer's dealership.

  • Fault Code: Failure code depending on the failure that has occurred. This field will be blank for a campaign claim.

  • Causal Part: Part serial number or part number of the causal part.

  • Date of Failure: Date on which the equipment/part failed. This field will be blank for a campaign claim.

  • Date of Repair: Date when the repair work was performed.

To view the claim details on a single page, double click on the claim number of that claim. The claim will be shown in the detailed page, Claim No. (n). For further information on the claim detail page, click here.

 

You can view the comments entered by the personnel requesting advice, in the Claim History section. You cannot edit any claim details for claims in your Advice Request folder. You can analyze the claim, attach documents to support your advice, provide comments on the claim and send it back to the OEM personnel who requested advice.

 

Actions

The following actions are allowed on a claim in your Advice Request folder:

  • Comments: Enter any comments you may have about the claim here. You can enter your advice here.

  • Cancel: Click on this button to close this page without saving any details entered by you.

  • Respond: Click on this button to send your response to the personnel who requested your advice.
