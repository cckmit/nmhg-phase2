INTRODUCTION

An advisor is personnel who provide advice to a processor or an admin reviewing the claim submitted by a dealer.

 

The Warranty Management application allows you to:

  • Analyze the claim and attach documents to support your advice.

  • Provide comments on the claim and send it back to the OEM personnel who requested advice.

  • Search claims and inventory items.
