MANAGE USER BUSINESS UNIT MAPPING

There are different business units. Each user is assigned to a business unit. A user may be assigned to one or more business units at the same time.

 

To assign a business unit to a user,

  • Click on Manage User Business Unit Mapping tab under the Settings tab on the left-hand pane.

  • Enter the login id of the user to whom you want to assign the business unit, click Search.

  • Select the checkbox against the user name.

  • Select the required business units from the available list of business units displayed under the Available Business Units column. You can select multiple business units by pressing the Ctrl key and simultaneously clicking on the business units.

  • Click on to move the selected business units under the Selected Business Units column. To remove a business unit from the Selected Business Units column, select the particular business unit and click on .

  • Click Update.
