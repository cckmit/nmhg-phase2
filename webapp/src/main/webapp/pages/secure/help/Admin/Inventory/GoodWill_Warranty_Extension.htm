Policy WARRANTY EXTENSION

A Warranty administrator can provide extensions to standard or extended warranty. These extensions are also known as Policy extensions and can be applied anytime.

 

To provide a Policy warranty extension,

  • Click on Policy Warranty Extension link under the Fleet Inventory Management tab on the left pane. The Policy Warranty Extension page on the right pane displays all the policies present in system.

  • Select any one of the warranty policy from the available list of policies.

  • Click on on Select Additional Inventories link.

  • Enter the serial number of the inventory or first few digits of the serial number, click Search. Select the inventories and click Select. You will find that some data is pre-populated on this page. The data is derived from the serial number of the inventory.
