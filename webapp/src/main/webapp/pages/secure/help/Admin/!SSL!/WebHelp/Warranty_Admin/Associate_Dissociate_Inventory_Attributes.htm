ASSOCIATE/DISSOCIATE INVENTORY ATTRIBUTES

Once an attribute has been created, you can associate it to any inventory. Perform the following steps to associate an attribute to an inventory.

  • Click on Additional Attributes tab under the Warranty Admin tab on the left-hand pane. Click on Associate/Dissociate Inventory Attributes tab under Additional Attributes. A Manage Attributes page will open on the right-hand side.

  • Click on the attribute you want to associate. You can view the attribute details that were selected while creating the attribute.

  • Click on button under the Associate Parts section.

  • Enter the Product Name to which you want to associate this attribute. The attribute will be associated to all the models present under that product. Apart from product type you can also specify a particular model number or job code or fault location to which you want to associate this attribute. You can also associate an attribute to multiple products by clicking on button. To delete a row, click on the option corresponding to that row.

  • Click Submit. You will receive a success message confirming your action. If you click Cancel, it will disregard the action and move you to the previous page.

You can also dissociate an attribute by clicking on the option corresponding to the product/inventory with which you want to dissociate the attribute. Once you click Submit, the attribute will no longer be associated to that product/inventory.

 

To view a list of stocks to which the attribute has been set, click on the PreDefined Stock Search/ PreDefined Retail Search tab under Inventory tab on the left-hand pane. Select the Product Type or Model Number to which you have associated the attribute, and click Search. You will see a list of all the inventories for which the attribute has been associated. Click on the inventory on which you want to file a claim. In the process of filing a claim, you will see an Enter Additional Attribute link corresponding to that inventory, under Truck Details section on page 2 of 2 of the Create Claim page. You can add any additional information here which may be text, date or number depending upon the data type selected while creating the attribute.
