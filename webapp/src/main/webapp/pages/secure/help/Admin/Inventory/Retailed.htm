Retailed Inventory

The Retailed folder contains detailed information on your merchandised inventory. The data in this folder allows you to efficiently search for an truck that you have sold to an end customer.

 

To view your retailed inventories,

  • Click on the Inventory tab on the left-hand pane. This reveals the folders under this tab, Stock and Retailed. Click on the Retailed folder.

  • Use the Inventory Query option to query for all inventories retailed by you. Click here to know more about inventory querying.

On the right-hand pane, you can see the Retailed Inventory table that lists your retailed inventory. For further information on the navigating through the table, see Lists.

 

The Retailed Inventory table provides the following information about each truck:

  • Serial Number: The serial number of the truck. This number is a unique identifier assigned to this truck.

  • Item Number: This is the number of the item to which this truck belongs. This number determines the unique class of product of which type the truck is.

  • Model: The model number of the product to which this truck belongs.

  • Product: The product to which the inventory item belongs.

  • Warranty Start Date: Date when this truck will be covered by the warranty policies associated with it. This data will not be displayed for an truck in your stock inventory.

  • Warranty End Date: Date when this truck will be out of warranty, i.e. the policies covering this truck will be inactivated. This data will not be shown for an truck in your stock inventory.

By default, details of the first truck in Retailed Inventory table will be displayed in the preview pane in a tabbed view.

 

truck Details

Click on an truck to view its details in the preview pane. To view the truck details on a single page, double click on the truck in the Retailed Inventory table. All details of the selected truck will be shown on the truck details page of that truck.

 

You can view the following data for the selected truck on in the preview pane and detail page: truck info, details of all transaction associated with this truck, a list of all warranty policies registered on this truck, a history of claims filed on this truck, and a list of pending service campaigns on this truck. Click here to know more about the truck details page.

 

Actions

The following actions are allowed on the trucks in your Retailed folder:

  • Truck Transfer: Clicking on this button brings up the Truck Transfer page for the selected truck. Click here to know more about truck transfer.

  • Extended Warranty Purchase: Clicking on this button brings up the Extended Warranty Purchase page for the selected truck. Click here to know more about extended warranty purchase.

  • Create Claim: Clicking on this button brings up the claim submission page for this truck. Click here to know more about submitting claims.

In addition to above actions, you can perform the below action when you click on the retailed inventory listed on the Retailed Inventory table.

  • Reports: Clicking on this page will open the Custom Reports page. You can fill these reports and submit it for this truck.
