Overdue Parts

After 20 days of WPRA generation (items available in Due Parts inbox), the returnable part list moves to Overdue Parts inbox. Then, the dealer must take the responsibility to ship the part.

To view the parts that are overdue to be returned to the OEM, click on the Part Returns tab on the left-hand pane. The tab will open to display the folders under it. Click on the Overdue Parts folder. On the right-hand pane, you can see the Overdue Parts table that lists all parts overdue to be returned to the OEM.

 

The functioning of the Overdue Parts folder is similar to that of the Due Parts folder, with an additional field, Overdue Days. The Overdue Days field shows the number of days since which this part is overdue for return. This is the number of days from the due date for this part and the current date. For details of other fields on the Overdue Parts page, refer Due Parts.
