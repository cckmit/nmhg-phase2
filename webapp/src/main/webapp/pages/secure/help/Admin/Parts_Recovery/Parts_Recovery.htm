Parts recovery

Each warehouse has a personnel who is in charge of shipping parts marked for recovery to the respective suppliers. The part shipper collects all parts marked for supply recovery from the bins in the warehouse and ships them to the location specified by the Supply Recovery Admin (SRA).

 

The Parts Recovery section is designed to help you manage the process of returning replaced parts that are requested back by the OEM. The part return information is sprucely maintained in different folders depending on the stage of their processing.

  • Awaiting Shipment

  • Part Not in Warehouse

  • Awaiting Shipment to Warehouse

  • Supplier Parts Claimed

  • Shipment Generated

  • Supplier Parts Shipped

To access any of these folders, you must click on that folder link. A new page, displaying the contents of the selected folder, will appear in the right-hand pane.
