PART SHIPPED NOT RECEIVED

The parts that are marked for shipment must be returned within the specified number of days. If the part marked shipped is not returned within the specified number of days, set by the admin, the claim will automatically move to the Part Shipped Not Received inbox as per the action selected by the admin under the Manage Business Configuration page. The admin can either set the action as Deny Claim or MovetoShippedNotReceivedInbox for all such claims. To know more about business configurations, click here.

 

To view the claims having parts marked for shipment but not received, click on Part Shipped Not Received under the Claims tab on the left-hand pane. For further information on navigating through the table, see Lists. Double click on the claim number to view the claim details.
