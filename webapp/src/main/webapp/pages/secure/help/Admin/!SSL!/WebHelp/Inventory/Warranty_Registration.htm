Delivery Report

Delivery Report is used to capture information about truck arrival, truck delivery and truck sale.

You can generate the DR for a truck by,

  • Clicking on the Delivery Report link in the Go To column on your Home page.

  • Clicking on Delivery Report under the Fleet Inventory Management folder present under the Inventory tab on the left had pane.

Any of the above actions will take you to the Delivery Report page.

 

To generate a Delivery Report, you will need to provide the details about the truck, end customer who is buying this truck, sales person associated with the sale of this truck and other marketing details on this page. This page is divided into various sections for ease of navigation. The fields under some sections on this page may be hidden. To view the hidden fields, click on the section header. This will expand the section showing all fields under it. Likewise, clicking on the section header of an expanded section collapses it hiding all fields under it.

 

New Customer

This section gathers information about the customer purchasing the item. The customer could be an individual or a company. You can use the customer address in the address book of the service provider who has retailed this inventory. You can either select an existing customer or create a new one in the address book of the service provider, and then go on to select the newly created customer.

  • The Customer Types available depends on the values which the Warranty Admin has configured.

  • If you want to create a new customer, then click on the Create Customer button on this window. You will be taken to the Create Customer page. For further information on creating new customers, click here. The newly created customer will appear in the customer list on the pop-up window.

  • To select a customer from the list, click on the Select link corresponding to the customer. The selected customer details are shown in the New Customer section.

Operator Information

This section gathers information about the operator of the item, if any. You can either select an existing operator or create a new one in the address book of the service provider, and then go on to select the newly created operator.

  • After selecting the customer type, click on the option alongside the customer field. A pop-up window will appear. This window will list all existing operator of the selected type, who are already registered in your address book.

  • If you want to create a new operator, then click on the Create Operator button on this window. You will be taken to the Create Operator page. For further information on creating new operators, click here. The newly created operator will appear in the customer list on the pop-up window.

  • To select a operator from the list, click on the Select link corresponding to the customer. The selected operator details are shown in the New Operator section.

Additional Information

This section captures additional information such as details about the dealer representative, maintenance contract,  etc.

  • Contract Code: Use the drop-down field to select the type of Contract Code, ie, Lease, Long Term Rental etc.

  • Dealer Representative: Specify the name of the Dealer Representative here.

  • Maintenance Contract: Use the drop-down field to select the type of Maintenance Contract, ie, Customer Representative, Dealer Representative etc.

  • Industry Code: Use the drop-down field to select the Industry Code, ie, Basic Service Industries, Manufacturing Industries etc.

Note: If you select the customer type as Dealer Rental, all the fields under the Additional Information section become non-editable.

Service Details

Under this section, enter the name of the Installing Dealer in the available text field.

Manage Documents

In this section you can attach binary documents, provided by the dealer. To attach a document, click on the Browse button to browse through your system for the document. All selected documents will be attached to the claim. To delete an entry, click on the option.

 

truck Information

This section captures details about the truck on which you are filing the delivery report. You can view/add the following details here:

  • Copy Install Date: Enter the date when the truck was installed.

  • Truck Serial Number: This field may be pre-populated with the serial number of the truck for which you are filing the Delivering Report. If this field is blank, then enter the serial number of the truck here. You will notice that this field supports auto-complete. The serial numbers of all trucks in your stock will be listed in the auto-complete option. You can select the appropriate serial number from the list.

  • Model: This is a read-only field and will show the model of the selected truck. This data is also derived from the serial number that you provide.

  • Product: This is a read-only field and will show the product name of the selected truck. This data is derived from the serial number that you provide.

  • Shipment Date: Enter the shipment date of the truck.

  • Build Date: This is a read-only field and will show the Build Date of the selected truck.

  • Delivery Date:  Select the Delivery Date of the truck.

  • Date of Installation: Enter the date when the truck was installed.

  • Hours on Truck: Specify the hours in service for this truck at the time of Delivery Report.

  • OEM: Select the OEM from the available list of OEMs in the drop-down.

Policy Details

You can view a list of policies that will be registered for this truck, in this section. All other standard warranty policies applicable for this truck will be listed after the 1 year standard policy.

 

Major Components

This section displays all the major components associated with the truck along with their details.

  • Component Serial Number: Serial Number of the major component.

  • Part Number: Part number of the component.

  • Description: A short description of the component

  • Part Fitted Date: Date on which the component was installed.

You can enter a major component detail by clicking on the and entering all the above information in the respective fields. To delete an entry, click on the option.

 

Marketing Information

This section captures all the marketing information, if any, associated with the truck.

 

Comments

In the Comments field, you can enter any additional comments you want to, with regard to this Delivery Report.

 

Actions

After entering relevant details in all mandatory fields, you can perform one of the following actions:

  • Save as Draft: If you want to save the data entered by you, then click this button. The entry will be listed in the Draft folder under the Delivery Report folder present under the Registration/Transfer tab on the left hand pane.

  • Submit: If you want to submit the Delivery Report on this truck, then click this button. The truck is now moved from your Stock folder to your Retailed folder.

A success message will be shown confirming your action.

 

Approval for Delivery Report

The truck is moved from your Stock folder to your Retailed folder depending upon the business unit flag set for the approval.

 

To set manual approval for DR:

  • Click on Manage Business Configurations tab under the Warranty Admin tab on the left-hand pane.

  • Click on Delivery Report under the Inventory section in the Manage Business Configurations page.

  • Select the radio button ‘Yes’ for “Delivery Report needs Manual Approval.” All Delivery Reports will now require manual approval.

  • Click Update.

Thus, if you submit a Delivery Report for any truck, you will still see the truck under the Stock folder. It will not move to the Retailed folder until the approval is obtained.

 

To view DR pending for approval,

  • Click on the Registration/Transfer tab on the left-hand pane. This reveals the folders under this tab, Delivery Report and Truck Transfer. Click on the Delivery Report folder. This folder will reveal the sub folder Pending for Approval which contains the list of stocks pending for approval.

  • On the Home page, click on Pending for Approval (Delivery Report) link which is shown as part of the Actions Folders column.

You can view/edit the Delivery Reports pending for approval by double clicking on the item.

 

Actions

After verifying all the relevant details, you can perform any of the following actions:

  • Accept: If you want to approve all the data entered under the DR for the stock, then click this button. The truck is now moved from your Stock folder to your Retailed folder.

  • Reject: If you want to reject the DR for this stock, then click this button. The truck is moved to the Rejected inbox of the dealer under the Delivery Report folder located under the Registration/Transfer tab.

  • Forwarded to Dealer:  If you find the information insufficient and you want to obtain more details before you approve that DR, then click this button. The truck is moved to the Forwarded to Dealer inbox. Once the dealer provides the requested information, the truck moves to the Replies inbox and finally when the DR is resubmitted for approval, it will appear in the Resubmitted inbox.

  • Delete: If you choose to delete the DR, then click on this button. The truck is moved to the Deleted inbox of the dealer under the Delivery Report folder located under the Registration/Transfer tab.
