PRICEMATRIX

The dealers are paid DCAP allowance as SEA or SCA based on accrual amount selected from Price Matrix. The price matrix is based on Dealer Category, DCAP Model code, currency and the shipment date. If there is no price setup, the accrual amount is set to zero.

 

To create a price matrix,

  • Click on Manage DCAP Pricing tab under Warranty Admin tab on the left-hand pane. This will reveal the sub-folders Create PriceMatrix and Search PriceMatrix. Click on Create PriceMatrix and enter the following details on the Manage DCAP Pricing page:

  • Currency: Enter the currency in which the unit was invoiced.

  • From Date and To Date: Enter the shipment date. The date should belong to the date range set up for the given DCAP Model code, Dealer Category and Currency.

  • Enter the price against the product for all the dealer types.

  • Split and enter the percentage figure for SEA and SCA against each product line. The total of split percentage should be 100%. For instance, if you enter 25 under SEA, then for SCA you should enter 75.

  • Click Save.

You can also view the price matrix available in the system for particular currency and for a specific date range.

 

To search a price matrix,

  • Click on Manage DCAP Pricing tab under Warranty Admin tab on the left-hand pane. This will reveal the sub-folders Create PriceMatrix and Search PriceMatrix. Click on Search PriceMatrix and enter the following details on the Manage DCAP Pricing page:

  • Currency: Enter the currency in which the unit was invoiced.

  • From Date and To Date: Enter the shipment date. The date should belong to the date range set up for the given DCAP Model code, Dealer Category and Currency.

  • Click Search.

You can also modify the price matrix by changing the split percentage between SEA and SCA or updating the price for a product line. Click Update to save any changes made to the price matrix.
