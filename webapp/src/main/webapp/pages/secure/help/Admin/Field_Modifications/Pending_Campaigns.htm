PENDING FIELD MODIFICATIONS

A service campaign is a service initiative by the OEM where a specific set of equipment is marked to be serviced even without the failure. When service campaigns need to be performed on some equipments, the Admin sets up the campaigns for all affected units, providing necessary details. If the service campaign needs to be performed on any equipment in your stock or retail, a notification of the same is listed in your Pending Field Modifications folder.

 

To view all pending service campaigns,

  • Click on the Pending Field Modifications link under the Action Folders on the Home page.

  • Click on the Field Modifications tab on the left-hand pane. The tab will open to reveal the folders under it. Click on the Pending Field Modifications folder.

Either of the above actions takes you to the Pending Field Modifications page. On this page you can see the Pending Field Modifications table that lists all claims forwarded by the processor requesting further information. For further information on navigating through the table, see Lists.

 

The Pending Claims table provides the following information about each campaign claim:

  • Serial Number: Serial number of the equipment on which the service campaign must be performed.

  • Campaign Number: Campaign code for this campaign.

  • Description: A short description of the campaign.

  • Start Date: Date when this campaign will commence.

  • End Date: Date till which this campaign will appear in the service provider's Pending Field Modification list.

By default, the details of the campaign will be displayed in tabbed view in the preview pane. To view the campaign details on a single page, double click on the campaign in the table. The Serial Number (n) page is shown. All information relevant to the selected campaign will be displayed on this page.

 

Actions

You can submit a field modification claim on this campaign after performing the suggested services on this equipment. To do this, click on the Create Field Modification Claim button on this page. You will be taken to the Field Modification Claim page where you can provide details of the campaign as performed by you and claim for the expenses incurred.
