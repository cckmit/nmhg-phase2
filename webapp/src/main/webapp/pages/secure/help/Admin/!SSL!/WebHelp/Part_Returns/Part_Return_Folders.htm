Part ReturnS Folders

Sometimes, a dealer may not have access to the Warranty Management system. In such cases, you can submit claims on behalf of the dealer and also manage the part returns related to such claims. The Part Returns section is designed to help you manage the process of returning replaced parts that are requested back by the OEM.

 

Information about part returns is presented in the following folders:

  • Prepare Due Parts

  • Due Parts

  • Overdue Parts

  • Shipment Generated

  • Parts Shipped

  • Rejected Parts

  • Claimed Parts Receipt

  • Required Parts Return

  • Required Parts From Dealer

  • WPRA Generated For Parts

  • Third Party Due Parts

To access any of these folders, you must click on that folder link. A new page, displaying the contents of the selected folder, will appear in the right-hand pane.
