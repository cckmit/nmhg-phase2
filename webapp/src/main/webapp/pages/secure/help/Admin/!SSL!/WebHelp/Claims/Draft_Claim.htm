DRAFT

The Warranty Management application, by default, automatically attempts to save any claims that you create but do not submit. You can save a new claim at any given time by clicking the Save button on the New Claim page. The claim will be stored in the Draft folder. You can complete and submit this claim at any later instance of time or discard it. The total number of claims in this folder will be indicated by the number in the parenthesis following the folder.

 

To view all draft claims,

  • Click on the Draft Claim link under the Action Folders column on your Home page.

  • Click on the Claims tab on the left-hand pane. The tab will open to reveal the folders under it. Click on the Draft Claim folder.

Either of the above actions takes you to the Draft Claim page. On this page you can see the Draft table that lists all claims saved as draft. For further information on navigating through the table, see Lists.

 

The Draft Claims table provides the following information about each draft claim:

  • Claim Number: Serial number of the claim.

  • Claim Type: Kind of claim. The claim can be of type machine, parts or campaign.

  • Dealer: Name of your dealership.

  • Fault Location: Failure code depending on the failure that has occurred. This field will be blank for a campaign claim.

  • Causal Part: Part serial number or part number of the causal part.

  • Date of Failure: Date on which the equipment/part failed. This field will be blank for a campaign claim.

  • Date of Repair: Date when the repair work was performed.

To view the claim details on a single page, double click on the claim number of that claim. The Claim No. (n) page is shown. All information relevant to the selected claim will be displayed under various sections.

 

You can add and edit data in various fields on this page.

 

Actions

You can perform the following actions on a claim in the Draft Claim folder,

  • Double click on a claim in the Draft Claims table. The claim will open in the Claim No. (n) page.

  • Enter relevant details in pending fields on this page.

  • Click Validate to validate and submit the claim. Click here for further details on claim validation.

  • If you wish to close this page without saving any changes made by you, click Cancel.

  • Click Delete to remove the claim from this folder.

  • Click Go to Page 1 to go to Page 1 of 2 of the claim.

  • Click Save to retain the changes made by you and save the claim in the Draft Claim folder.

A success message will be shown confirming your action. The message will hide itself and the current page will be closed.
