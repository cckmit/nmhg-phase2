manage claim payment

Managing claim payment consists of setting up the rules and configurations which enable the system to calculate the claim amount that needs to be reimbursed to the service provider. A claim consists of various cost categories such as replaced parts, labor and travel involved, and freight expenses that were needed during a warranted repair/servicing of an truck. The OEM can associate various modifiers, for each of these cost categories. The payment for a claim depends on the policy covering the truck and the cost categories selected in the associated payment definition.

 

The Warranty Management application allows you to efficiently create and manage the application of modifiers to various cost categories.

 

Click on the Manage Claim Payment folder. You will find the following links here:

  • Create Claim Payment Modifiers

  • Configure Claim Payment Definition
