MANAGE SUPPLIERS

A supplier is a person representing the manufacturer who supplies parts to the OEM.

 

You can view and update the supplier details by clicking on Manage Suppliers folder under the Contract Admin tab on the left-hand pane. The Supplier table on the right-hand pane displays the supplier number, name, contact person name and address of the supplier. See Lists for more information on navigating through this table.

 

If you double click on any entry in this table, all details relevant to that supplier will be displayed on a new page, Supplier Number <n>. You can view/edit the following details in the Supplier Number <n> page:

 

Supplier Details

  • Supplier Number: It is the unique number associated with the supplier.

  • Name: The name of the supplier.

  • Preferred Location Type: Retail/Business

  • Address: Address of the supplier.

  • Preferred Currency: Currency selected by the supplier.

Supplier Items

  • Part Number: Part number of the part.

  • Description: A short description of the part.

  • Supplier Part Number: Part number of the part supplied by the supplier.

  • Supplier Part Description: A short description of the part supplied by the supplier.

  • From and To Date: Date range during which the supplier covers the part.

You can also create a supplier and associate a part to the supplier.

To create a supplier,

  • Click on Create Supplier button in the listing toolbar.

  • Enter the details under the Supplier Details section.

  • Click on the Add Row button to add a supplier part under the Supplier Items section. To delete any row click the option corresponding to that row.

  • Click Add Supplier to add a supplier to the list. Click Cancel to close the page without saving any details entered on this page.
