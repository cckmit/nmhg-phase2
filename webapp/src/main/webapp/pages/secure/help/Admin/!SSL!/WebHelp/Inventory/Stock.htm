STOCK Inventory

The Stock folder contains detailed information about your inventory at hand. The data in this folder allows you to efficiently search for an truck that you have in your stock, register warranties on these trucks and also submit claims on these trucks.

 

To view your stock inventories,

  • Click on the Inventory tab on the left-hand pane. This reveals the folders under this tab, Stock (Count of Trucks) and Retailed. Click on the Stock (Count of Trucks) folder.

  • Use the Inventory Query option to query for all inventories in your stock. Click here for more information on inventory querying.

On the right-hand pane, you can see the Stock Inventory table that lists your stock inventory. For further information on navigating through the table, see Lists.

The Stock Inventory table provides the following information about each truck:

  • Serial Number: The serial number of the inventory item. This number is a unique identifier assigned to that inventory item.

  • Product: The product to which the inventory item belongs.

  • Model: The model number of the product to which the inventory item belongs.

  • Item Number: This is the number of the item to which the inventory item belongs. This number determines the unique class of product of which type the inventory item is.

  • Item Description: The description of the inventory item.

  • Shipment Date: The date when it was shipped to the service provider.

  • Unit Age: The number of months for which the inventory is in stock.

truck Details

To view a particular truck detail, double click on that entry in the Stock Inventory table. All details of the selected truck will be shown on the truck details page of that truck.

 

You can view the following data for the selected truck in the detail page: truck info, a list of major components, owning dealer information, details of the transaction of this truck from the OEM to you, a list of claims filed on this truck, and a list of pending service campaigns on this truck. Click here to know more about the truck details page.

 

Actions

The following actions are allowed on the trucks in your Stock folder:

  • Delivery Report: Clicking on this button brings up the Delivery Report page for this truck. Click here to know more about Delivery Report.

  • Dealer to Dealer Transfer: Clicking on this button brings up the Stock inventory transfer page allowing the user to transfer the Inventory to another Dealership. Click here to know more about Dealer to Dealer Transfer.

  • Create Claim: Clicking on this button brings up the claim submission page for this truck. Click here to view more details on submitting claims.

In addition to above actions, you can perform the below two actions when you click on the stock inventory listed on the Stock Inventory table.

  • Mark as Scrapped: Clicking on this button brings up scrap inventories page for this inventory. Click here to view more details on scrap inventories.

  • Reports: Clicking on this page will open the Custom Reports page. You can fill these reports and submit it for this truck.
