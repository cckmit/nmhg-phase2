InTRODUCTION

When a service provider files a claim, the part causing the failure in the equipment is returned to the warehouse. This part may be sourced from a supplier and may be covered under a supplier contract. All such parts need to be returned to the supplier for recovery and reimbursement, as stated in the contract covering it. The Supplier Recovery Admin (SRA) is the OEM personnel responsible for managing recovery of parts from the sourcing suppliers. This includes shipping the parts to the supplier if needed and negotiating with the supplier on the final recovery amount.

 

The Warranty Management application allows you to:

  • Manage supply recovery process

  • Create and maintain supplier contracts

  • Search for claims, inventory items and parts

The Warranty Management application thus allows you to efficiently manage the recovery process and claim reimbursement from the supplier whose part caused the failure in the equipment.
