Claim VALIDATION

While filing a claim, you are required to validate the claim before submission. When you validate a claim, the Warranty Registration applications performs all the system validations and entry validations on your claim and notifies you of any errors in the claim. The results of the validation are shown on the Validate pop-up window.

 

When you click on the Validate button on the claim submission page, the Validate pop-up window is shown. You can view the system validation errors, entry validation errors, applicable policy details, replaced part details, labor and travel charges claimed, and the payment details on this window.

 

System Validation Errors

During validation, if your claim had any system validation errors, then you will not be allowed to submit that claim unless you entry valid data in the erroneous fields. All system validation errors for your claim will be shown on top of the Validate window.

 

Only the Edit Claim button is shown on this window. When you click on this button, all fields with erroneous data will be highlighted in red to indicate the error. Click on the icon alongside a highlighted field to view the error message.

 

Validate Window

The following data will be shown on the Validate window if there are no system validation errors:

  • Entry Validation (errors and warnings): These errors and warnings are the result of validating the claim against the entry validation rules. The errors are shown first followed by warnings. If there are any errors, you cannot submit the claim before you enter valid data in the erroneous fields. However, if there are only warnings, then you can submit the claim.

  • Applicable Policy: The name of the policy applicable for your claim is displayed here. This is a hyperlink to the policy details page. Click here to know more about the policy details page.

In case no policy registered on this equipment is valid for this claim, then the message “Out of Warranty” is shown here. If you are filing a claim on a stock inventory, then you will see the message “In Stock” displayed here.

  • Replaced Part Details: All parts entered by you in the Parts Replaced section of the claim will be shown here. The unit price for each part is also shown against that part.

  • Labor and Travel Rates: All the rates and modifier values associated with the labor and travel amounts claimed by you, will be displayed here in separate rows.

  • Payment Details: You can view the detailed payment structure for your claim. The following columns are shown:

  • Cost Category: You can see names of all cost categories claimed by you here. The various cost categories include: Parts Replaced, Outside Parts and Services, Labor Amount, Travel Amount, Freight Charges and Claim Amount.

  • Amount Asked: The amount claimed by you for each of the cost categories is shown here.

Actions

The following actions are shown on this window:

  • Edit Claim: Clicking on this button will take you back to the claim submission page. You can edit any details you want on this page.

  • Submit Claim: This button is shown only when there are no system validation and entry validation errors. Clicking on this button will submit your claim for further processing.

When a claim is successfully submitted you can see the submission success message on the claim submission page. The message hides itself after some time and closes the tab.
