InTRODUCTION

A District Service Manager (DSM) is an OEM personnel who supervises all service providers under his/her dealership. Every dealership may have one or two DSMs.

 

Sometimes, dealers may send their claims for approval to you. This is referred to as Service Manager Review (SMR). You can analyze the claim, verify the reason given by the dealer for submitting the claim for SMR, and then approve or deny the claim. The dealer can still submit a claim denied by you, to the OEM for reimbursement.

 

Likewise, a processor or an admin can send a claim to you requesting advice. You can analyze the claim and provide suitable advice.

 

The Warranty Management application allows you to:

  • Verify claims submitted to you for SMR.

  • Accept or Deny SMR claims.

  • Provide advice to processors or admin when requested.

  • Search claims and inventory items.
