Inventory

Inventory refers to a collection of trucks. All trucks at hand are termed as Stock Inventory and all trucks sold to end users are termed as Retailed Inventory. As a Processor, you can view the inventory in the stock and retail of any dealer by querying for it.

 

On the left-hand pane, click on the Inventory tab. The tab opens to reveal the folders under it:

  • Stock

  • Retailed

To access any of these folders, you must click on that folder link. A new page, displaying the list of inventories, will appear on the right-hand pane. Double-click on the inventory to view the details.

 

You can perform the following actions on the queried inventory items:

View the Truck Details page of an inventory.

Perform Dealer to Dealer Transfer on the inventory items.

Submit claims on the inventory items.

Generate Delivery Report for the inventory.

Generate Truck Transfer Report for the inventory.

Perform Extended warranty purchase on the inventory items.
