TRUCK Transfer Report

Truck transfer occurs when a retailed truck is sold from one end customer to another end customer. A Truck Transfer Report (TTR) is generated when such a transaction occurs. While doing a truck transfer, all data such as current and new customer details, policies applied and sales details are captured.

 

You can generate the TTR for an truck by,

  • Clicking on the Truck Transfer button displayed on top of the page of the following screens:

  • Retailed inventory listing

  • Inventory Search result (for Retailed)

  • Inventory Quick search

  • Click on the Truck Transfer link available in the Go To section of the home page.

  • Clicking on Truck Transfer under the Fleet Inventory Management folder present under the Inventory tab on the left had pane.

Any of the above actions will take you to the Truck Transfer page. To generate an TTR, you will need to provide the details about the truck, current and new end customers associated with this truck, and other marketing details on the Truck Transfer page. This page is divided into various sections for ease of navigation. The fields under some sections on this page may be hidden. To view the hidden fields, click on the section header. This will expand the section showing all fields under it. Likewise, clicking on the section header of an expanded section collapses it hiding all fields under it.

 

New Customer

This section gathers information about the customer purchasing the item. The customer could be a dealer, direct customer, end customer or a National Account.

  • You can enter the customer information by selecting the Customer Type and then clicking on the option alongside the customer field. A pop-up window will appear. This window will list all existing customers of the selected type, who are already registered in your address book.

  • If you want to create a new customer, then click on the Create Customer button on this window. You will be taken to the Create Customer page. For further information on creating new customers, click here.

  • The newly created customer will appear in the customer list on the pop-up window.

  • You can also edit the details of an existing customer by clicking on the Edit link corresponding to that customer. For further details on editing customer information, click here.

  • To select a customer from the list, click on the Select link corresponding to the customer.

  • The selected customer details are shown in the New Customer section.

Operator Information

Enter the operator information same as you have entered New Customer information.

Service Details

Enter the details of the installing dealer here.

Manage Documents

Browse and add any documents, if desired.

Truck Information

This section captures details about the truck on which you are submitting the TTR. You can view/add the following details here:

  • Copy Install Date: Enter the Copy Install Date here.

  • Serial Number: This field may be pre-populated with the serial number of the truck for which you are submitting TTR. If this field is blank, then enter the serial number of the truck here. You will notice that this field supports auto-complete. The serial numbers of all trucks in the Retailed folder of all service providers, associated with the OEM, will be listed in the auto-complete option. You can select the appropriate serial number from the list.

  • Product: This is a read-only field and will show the product name of the selected truck. This data is derived from the serial number that you provide.

  • Model: This is a read-only field and will show the model of the selected truck. This data is also derived from the serial number that you provide.

  • Transfer Date: Select the date when this truck was transferred to the new customer. You can also manually enter the date in this field.

  • Date of Installation: Select the date when this truck was installed for the new customer. You can also manually enter the date in this field.

  • Hours on Machine: Specify the hours in service for this truck at the time of transfer.

  • VIN/Truck Number:Specify the truck number here.

  • Truck/Trailer number: Specify the Truck/Trailer number here.

  • OEM: Select the OEM from the drop-down.

Warranty Coverage

Enter the warranty coverage details in this section.

Major Components

Marketing Information

The details in this section are automatically populated depending on the truck serial number.

Manage Documents

Browse and add any documents, if desired.

Comments

In the Comments field, you can enter any additional comments you want to, with regard to this truck transfer.

Actions

After entering relevant details in all mandatory fields, you can perform one of the following actions:

  • Save as Draft: If you want to save the Truck Transfer page, then click this button.

  • Submit: If you want to submit the truck transfer details, then click this button. The truck is now moved from the current customer to the new customer.

Note: You can also file an TTR for multiple inventories by clicking the button.

 

A success message will be shown confirming your action. You can view the TTR filed information for that inventory in the Transaction History section for any inventory. For further details on the truck details page, click here.

 

Approval for TTR

The TTR is filed for an truck depending upon your selection whether manual approval is required for filing an TTR or not.

 

Perform the following steps to set manual approval for TTR:

  • Click on Manage Business Configurations tab under the Warranty Admin tab on the left-hand pane.

  • Click on Transfers (TTR, D2D) under the Inventory section in the Manage Business Configurations page.

  • Select the radio button ‘Yes’ for “Is Manual Approval Flow required for TTR.” All TTR filings will now require manual approval.

  • Click Update. A successful message is received confirming your action.

 Note: You can file an TTR only for those trucks for which already DR is done.

 

To view TTR pending for approval:

  • On the Home page, click on Pending for Approval (Truck Transfer) link which is shown as part of the Actions Folders column or click on Pending for Approval under the Registration/Transfer tab on the left-hand pane.

  • Double-click on the truck details to view/edit or take any other action.

Actions

After verifying the relevant details, you can perform the following actions:

  • Accept: If you want to approve all the data entered, then click this button. The truck transfer is now approved.

  • Reject: If you want to reject the TTR for this truck, then click this button. The truck is moved to the Rejected inbox of the dealer under the Truck Transfer folder located under the Registration/Transfer tab.

  • Forwarded to Dealer: If you find the information insufficient and you want to obtain more details before you approve that warranty, then click this button. The truck is moved to the Forwarded to Dealer inbox of the dealer under the Truck Transfer folder located under the Registration/Transfer tab.

  • Delete: If you choose to delete the DR, then click on this button. The truck is moved to the Deleted inbox of the dealer under the Truck Transfer folder located under the Registration/Transfer tab.
