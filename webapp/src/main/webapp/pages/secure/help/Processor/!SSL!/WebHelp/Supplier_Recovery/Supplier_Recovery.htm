SUPPLIER RECOVERY

Every time an truck covered by a warranty policy fails, the service provider of that truck performs necessary repairs and claims the incurred expenses from the OEM. The claim filed by the service provider specifies the part which caused the failure in the truck. If the causal part of a claim was sourced from a supplier, and if that part is covered under a supplier contract, then the OEM can recover the entire claim amount from the supplier, as mentioned in the contract. This process of recovering the claim amount from the supplier whose part caused the failure in the trucks, is known as Supplier Recovery.

 

To manage the supplier recovery for supplier sourced parts, you are provided with the following links under the Supplier Recovery tab:

  • Claims Due For Recovery

  • New

  • On Hold

  • Not for Recovery Request

  • Not for Recovery Response

  • Transferred

  • Claims Under Recovery

  • Disputed

  • Accepted

  • Awaiting Supplier Response

  • Not For Recovery

  • Multi Claim Maintenance

Click on a folder to view the details of parts in it. A new page will be shown on the right-hand pane displaying information of parts in the selected folder.

 

The Pending Recovery Initiation inbox link is present under the Claims tab on the left hand pane. It is also present under Action Folders on the Home Page. For more details, click here.
