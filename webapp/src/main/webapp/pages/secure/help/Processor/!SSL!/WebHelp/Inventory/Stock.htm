STOCK Inventory

The Stock folder contains detailed information about your inventory at hand. The data in this folder allows you to efficiently search for equipment that you have in your stock, register warranties on these equipments and also submit claims on these equipments.

 

To view your stock inventories,

  • Click on the Inventory tab on the left-hand pane. This reveals the folders under this tab, Stock and Retailed. Click on the Stock folder.

  • Use the Inventory Query option to query for all inventories in your stock. Click here for more information on inventory querying.

On the right-hand pane, you can see the Stock Inventory table that lists your stock inventory. For further information on navigating through the table, see Lists.

 

The Stock Inventory table provides the following information about each equipment:

  • Serial Number: The serial number of the inventory item. This number is a unique identifier assigned to that inventory item.

  • Product: The product to which the inventory item belongs.

  • Model: The model number of the product to which the inventory item belongs.

  • Item Number: This is the number of the item to which the inventory item belongs. This number determines the unique class of product of which type the inventory item is.

  • Shipment Date: The date when it was shipped to the service provider.

  • Machine Age: The number of months for which the inventory is in stock

Equipment Details

Click on an equipment to view its details in the preview pane. To view the equipment details on a single page, double click on the equipment in the Stock Inventory table. All details of the selected equipment will be shown on the equipment details page of that equipment.

 

You can view the following data for the selected equipment in the preview pane and the detail page: equipment info, a list of major components, details of the transaction of this equipment from the OEM to you, a list of claims filed on this equipment, and a list of pending service campaigns on this equipment. Click here to know more about the equipment details page.

 

Actions

The following actions are allowed on the equipments in your Stock folder:

  • Warranty Registration: Clicking on this button brings up the warranty registration page for this equipment. Click here to know more about registering warranties.

  • Dealer to Dealer Transfer: Clicking on this button brings up the Stock inventory transfer page allowing the user to transfer the Inventory to another Dealership. Click here to know more about Dealer to Dealer Transfer.

  • Create Claim: Clicking on this button brings up the claim submission page for this equipment. Click here to view more details on submitting claims.

In addition to above actions, you can perform the below two actions when you click on the stock inventory listed on the Stock Inventory table.

  • Mark as Scrapped: Clicking on this button brings up scrap inventories page for this inventory. Click here to view more details on scrap inventories.

  • Reports: Clicking on this page will open the Custom Reports page. You can fill these reports and submit it for this equipment.
