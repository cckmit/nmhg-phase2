WAITING FOR LABOR

The Waiting for Labor inbox includes all the Free of Cost (FOC) claims created against those parts that are replaced on a machine by a dealer during the warranty period. Free of cost parts is a method of strengthening warranty support in business and it basically means that any parts on a machine which fails in warranty period will be provided free of cost by business.

 

The creation of the FOC claim in particular user's 'Waiting for Labor' inbox depends on whether the dealer is filing an order for himself or on behalf of a third party. There are three possible scenarios:

  • If the dealer places the order of him/herself in the aftermarket system, the FOC claim created will be displayed in the dealer's own 'Waiting for Labor' inbox.

  • If the dealer files an order in the aftermarket system on behalf of a third party which is already setup in Tavant, the FOC claim will be created in the third party user's 'Waiting for Labor' inbox if there is a valid login for that third party.

  • If the dealer files an order in the aftermarket system on behalf of a third party which is not setup in Tavant, the FOC claim will be created in the internal user's 'Waiting for Labor' inbox who has appropriate permissions to work on FOC claims.

These FOC claims contain all the details of removed parts and replaced parts as provided in the aftermarket system by dealer. Dealer/Third Party/Warranty Processor can open the claim from this inbox and add more parts, labor and miscellaneous cost details and submit the claim for manual processing. Once submitted, the claim will process to automatic approval/acceptance or manual review based on rules evaluation.

 

Note: If the dealer does not take any action on the FOC claim within the window period configured by warranty admin, the claim get automatically submitted for processing.
