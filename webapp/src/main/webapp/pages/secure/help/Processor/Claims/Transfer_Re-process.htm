transfer/re-PROCESS

You can also transfer or re-process a claim directly by clicking the Transfer/Re-process link present under the Multi Claim Maintenance folder under the Claims tab. You can search for the claims by providing a phrase describing the business condition or create a new business condition.

 

You can search for positive results for a set of conditions that you define on this page, or you can also search for a negation of the set of conditions defined on this page. To know more about creating and editing existing business conditions, click here.

 

The page refreshes to show the search results in a tabular format.

 

You can select the desired claims and perform any one of the below actions:

  • Transfer to: Select this action to transfer the claim to another processor for manual adjudication. Use the drop-down field following this action to select an authorized processor or warranty administrator to whom you want to transfer this claim. The claim will now appear in the 'Transferred' inbox of the processor to whom the claim has been transferred.

  • Re-process: Select this action to re-process the claim.

Depending upon you selection, the claim will be processed once the Submit button is clicked.
