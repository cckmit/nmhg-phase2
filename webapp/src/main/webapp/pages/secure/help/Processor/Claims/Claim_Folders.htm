claim Folders

After a claim is submitted by the service provider, the claim is passed through the claims processing engine. The engine runs a set of rules which determines whether the claim can be paid automatically or not. If the claim fails to go through the processing engine successfully, it is assigned to a claims processor for manual adjudication.

 

As a claims processor, you can then decide to accept or reject a claim after thorough verification. You are allowed to change any of the claim data and also pass it on to other users within the OEM and the service provider who submitted the claim, to gather more information.

 

By default, the folders under the Claims section will be displayed in the left-hand pane when you successfully login to the Warranty Management application. As a processor, you have various folders under the Claim tab to allow you to efficiently manage claim processing:

  • New

  • Appealed

  • Forward Internally

  • Forward Externally

  • On Hold

  • Replies

  • Transferred

  • Transfer/Re-process

To access any of these folders, you must click on that folder link. A new tab, displaying the contents of the selected folder, will appear in the right-hand pane.
