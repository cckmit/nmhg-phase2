PENDING AUTHORIZATION

When a claim is sent for review, the processor receives it in his/her "processor review" inbox. The system displays an option "Approve and transfer to next user" that indicates that the user approves the claim but wishes to transfer it to a user who has the authority to approve the claimed amount. The system then sends the claim into the next user's Pending Authorization inbox.

 

To view the claims that are pending for approval, click on Pending Authorization under the Claims tab on the left-hand pane. For further information on navigating through the table, see Lists. Double click on the claim number and perform actions similar to the earlier Processor - Put on Hold, Request Information from Dealer, Request for Advice, Transfer to, Accept, Deny, Re-Process, Approve and transfer to next user.
