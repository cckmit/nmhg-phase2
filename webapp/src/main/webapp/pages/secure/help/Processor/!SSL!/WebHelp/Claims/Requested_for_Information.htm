While reviewing a claim, if you need more information on any item in the claim, you can send the claim back to the dealer who submitted it and request for further information. The claim will be moved to the dealer's inbox and a copy of the same will be moved to your Forwarded Externally or Forward Internally folder, for your reference.

 

To view all claims for which you have requested information from the filing dealer, click on the Claims tab on the left-hand pane. The tab will open to reveal the folders under it. Click on the Forwarded Externally or Forward Internally folder. You can see the Information Requested table that lists all claims forwarded to the respective dealer, requesting further information. For further information on navigating through the table, see Lists.

 

You can see the following details for each claim in the Information Requested table:

  • Claim Number: Serial number of the claim.

  • Claim Type: Kind of claim. The claim can be of type machine, parts or campaign.

  • Dealer: Name of your dealership.

  • Fault Code: Failure code depending on the failure that has occurred. This field will be blank for a campaign claim.

  • Causal Part: Part serial number or part number of the causal part.

  • Date of Failure: Date on which the truck/part failed. This field will be blank for a campaign claim.

  • Date of Repair: Date when the repair work was performed.

To view the claim details on a single page, double click on the claim number of that claim. The Claim No. (n) page is shown. All information relevant to the selected claim will be displayed under various sections. You cannot edit any details on this claim.
