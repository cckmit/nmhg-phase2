FORWARDED

The claims submitted by you may be assigned to a processor for manual review. The Forwarded folder contains the claims that have been forwarded by the processor reviewing your claim, for further clarifications or need of further information. The total number of claims in this folder will be indicated by the number in the parenthesis following the folder.

 

To view all claims sent you requesting information, click on the Claims tab on the left-hand pane. The tab will open to reveal the folders under it. Click on the Forwarded folder. On this page you can see the Forwarded Claims table that lists all claims forwarded by the processor requesting further information. For further information on navigating through the table, see Lists.

 

The Forwarded Claims table provides the following information about each forwarded claim:

  • Claim Number: Serial number of the claim.

  • Claim Type: Kind of claim. The claim can be of type machine, parts or campaign.

  • Dealer: Name of your dealership.

  • Fault Code: Failure code depending on the failure that has occurred. This field will be blank for a campaign claim.

  • Causal Part: Part serial number or part number of the causal part.

  • Date of Failure: Date on which the truck/part failed. This field will be blank for a campaign claim.

  • Date of Repair: Date when the repair work was performed.

To view the claim details on a single page, double click on the claim number of that claim. The Claim No. (n) page is shown. All information relevant to the selected claim will be displayed under various sections. You cannot edit any details on this claim.

 

Actions

The following actions are allowed on a claim in this folder:

  • Attach documents: You can attach binary documents, provided by the dealer, in the Manage Documents section. To attach a document, click on the option. A new row is shown under this section. Click on the Browse button to navigate through your system for the document. Click on Attach to attach the document to your claim.

  • Comments: Enter any clarifications you want to provide to the processor.

  • Cancel: Click on this button to close this page without saving any details entered by you.

  • Respond: Click on this button to submit your response to the processor.

Depending on the action taken by you, a success message will be shown confirming your action. The message will hide itself and the current page will be closed.
