Pending FIELD Product Improvement

A service campaign is a service initiative by the OEM where a specific set of truck is marked to be serviced even without the failure. When service campaigns need to be performed on some trucks, the Admin sets up the campaigns for all affected units, providing necessary details.

Pending Field Product Improvement

If the service campaign needs to be performed on any truck in your stock or retail, a notification of the same is listed in your Pending Field Product Improvement folder.

To view all pending service campaigns,

  • Click on the Pending Field Product Improvement link under the Action Folders on the Home page.

  • Click on the Field Product Improvement tab on the left-hand pane. The tab will open to reveal the folders under it. Click on the Pending Field Product Improvement folder.

Either of the above actions takes you to the Pending Field Product Improvement page. On this page, you can see the Pending Field Product Improvement table that lists all claims forwarded by the processor requesting further information. For further information on navigating through the table, see Lists.

 

The Pending Claims table provides the following information about each campaign claim:

  • Serial Number: Serial number of the truck on which the service campaign must be performed.

  • Field Product Improvement Code: The Field Product Improvement code for this campaign.

  • Field Product Improvement Description: A short description of the campaign.

  • Field Product Improvement Start Date: Date when this campaign will commence.

  • Field Product Improvement End Date: Date till which this campaign will appear in the service provider's Pending Field Product Improvement list.

  • Customer Name: the name of the customer for this Field Product Improvement campaign.

  • Campaign Class: The class of the campaign.

  • Model: The model number of the truck.

  • Dealer: The name of the dealer for this Field Product Improvement campaign.

  • Field Mod Age: The age of this campaign in days.

  • Status: The status of the campaign whether active or inactive.

To view a campaign details, double click on the campaign in the table. The Serial Number (n) page is shown. All information relevant to the selected campaign will be displayed on this page.

Actions

Create Field Product Improvement Claim

You can submit a Field Product Improvement claim on this campaign after performing the suggested services on this truck. To do this, click on the Create Field Product Improvement Claim button on this page. You will be taken to the Field Product Improvement Claim page where you can provide details of the campaign as performed by you and claim for the expenses incurred. Click here to know more about submitting a campaign claim.

To search for a Field Product Improvement claim, click here.

Update Field Product Improvement Status

You can also update the status of a Field Product Improvement campaign on individual trucks. To do this, click Update Field Product Improvement Status. This displays additional fields on this page which are explained below:

  • Reason: This drop down displays the managed list of values. Based on reason code selected by you, a set of instructions is displayed. These instructions help you in providing all required information (different based on status code).

  • Comments: Enter your comments here.

  • Attach Documents: Attach relevant documents with this campaign.

  • View Action History: For each status update, the action history is captured. As part of action history, the following details are  captured in the Field Mod Update Action History page:

  • User

  • Date Last Modified

  • Action performed

  • Reason For Update

  • Comments

After entering information in the required fields, click Submit.

Field Product Improvement Update Requests

The FPI campaign administrator can accept or deny the status change requested by the dealer. If accepted, the Field Product Improvement campaign is updated to 'Complete'.

This inbox displays the list of pending FPI update requests in read only view. This list has the same set of columns as Outstanding Field Product Improvement listing.

Denied Field Product Improvement Update Requests

If denied, the Field Product Improvement campaign remains Active and pending. In this case, you can re-submit the same FPI update request, if desired.

This inbox displays the list of FPI update requests denied by FPI administrator. This list has the same set of columns as Outstanding Field Product Improvement listing.
