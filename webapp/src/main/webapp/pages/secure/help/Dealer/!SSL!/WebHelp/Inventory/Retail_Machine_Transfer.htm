RETAIL Truck Transfer

Retail Truck Transfer (RTT) is the process of transferring retail inventories from one dealership to another dealership.

 

To file a RTT,

  • Click on the Inventory tab on the left-hand pane. This reveals the folders under this tab, Stock, Retail Truck Transfer. Click on the Retail Truck Transfer folder or Go to the Home page, click on the Inventory link under Quick Search. Enter the serial number of the inventory on which you want to file a RTT and select Retail Truck Transfer to continue.

  • Click on Select Additional Inventories link on the right-hand side of the page.

  • Enter the serial number of the truck or first few digits of the serial number, click Search. If you want to file RTT on multiple inventories, select the inventories and click Select. You will find that some data is pre-populated on this page. The data is derived from the serial number of the truck on which you are generating the RTT.

  • Click on Perform Transfer to input the new dealer details.

  • Enter the Dealer Name or Dealer Number to search dealer.

  • Click Select Dealer. A RTT details page will open reflecting information for the inventory selected for RTT and also the details for both the previous dealer and the new dealer selected.

  • Click Confirm.

You can also view a new transaction added under the Transaction History section. This describes the transfer of the dealership of the truck from the current owner to the new dealer. For further details on the truck details page, click here.
