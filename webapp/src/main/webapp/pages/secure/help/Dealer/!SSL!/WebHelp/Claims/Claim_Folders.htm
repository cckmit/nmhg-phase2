claim Folders

The folders under the Claims tab will be displayed in the left-hand pane when you successfully log in to the Warranty Management application.

 

This section allows you to maintain your draft, forwarded and DSM response claims. The folders provided in the Claims tab:

  • Draft

  • Forwarded

  • Service Manager Response

  • Claim Failure Reports

To access any of these folders, you must click on that folder link. A new page, displaying the contents of the selected folder, will appear in the right-hand pane.
