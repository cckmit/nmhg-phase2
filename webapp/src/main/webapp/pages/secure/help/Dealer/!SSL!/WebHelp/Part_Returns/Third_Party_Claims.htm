THIRD PARTY CLAIMS

Third party means unauthorized vendors who often do repairs on the machines sold to various end customers, either because the business does not have an authorized dealer in the respective area or third party is the most viable option for an end customer. Thus, the claims filed by the authorized dealer on behalf of the third party are called Third Party Claims.

 

To create a Third Party claim,

  • Click on Create Third Party Claim link under the Go To section on the Home page. A new page, Third Party Claim is shown. Select the claim type from the drop-down.

Note: You can create a third party claim only on Machine and Field Modification claims.

  • Click on Select Third Party link to enter the third party details. You can enter the third party number or name or first few digits/characters for search.

  • Click Search.

  • Click on the Select link against your third party.

Depending upon the claim type selection, enter the relevant details and click on Continue >>. To more about claim submission, click here.
