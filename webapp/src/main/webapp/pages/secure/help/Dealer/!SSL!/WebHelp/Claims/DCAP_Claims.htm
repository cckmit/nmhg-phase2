DCAP CLAIMS

DCAP refers to Dealer Categorization Allowance Payment. DCAP claims give the opportunity to a dealer to essentially claim two types of allowance depending on set-up:

  • SEA: It is a payment category that may or may not be used by your business unit.

  • SCA: It is a payment category that may or may not be used by your business unit.

Once a DCAP claim is submitted and passes all the validations, the system automatically accepts the claim and sends it for payment notification.

 

To submit a DCAP claim,

  • Click on Submit DCAP Claims on the Home page under the Go To section or, click on the Submit DCAP Claim button from the Equipment Info page.

  • Select the dealer name.

  • Click on the button and enter the serial number of the equipment on which you want to file a DCAP claim. You can also file multiple DCAP claims by clicking on the button. To delete any row click the option corresponding to that row.

  • Select the type of DCAP claim, SEA or SCA.

  • Click Submit.

To search a DCAP claim,

  • Click on DCAP Claim Search under the DCAP Search Folders located under the Claims tab on the left-hand pane or

  • Click on DCAP claim under Quick Search on the Home page or

  • Click on Predefined Search (DCAP Claim) link under the Common Actions on the Home page.

Any of the above actions will take you to DCAP Claim Search page.

  • Select the parameter on the basis of which you can search the DCAP claim. You can select/enter either one parameter or as many as parameters to narrow your claim search.

  • Click Search.

It will display all the DCAP claims along with their details.

 

You can also search a DCAP claim using a search query,

  • Click on Define Search Query under the DCAP Search Folders located under the Claims tab on the left-hand pane.

  • Click on the  button on the right-hand side under Create Business Condition section.

  • Select the search criteria from the available list of business conditions such as Created Date, Claim Number, Claim Type, etc. and enter the text with which you want to search. For Instance, if you want to search for DCAP claim numbers, select Claim Number from the list and use the identifier ‘starts with’ and enter ‘D’ in the text field.

  • Click Execute to view your search result.

You can also view your search criteria by clicking on the  button on the listing toolbar. It will display a phrase describing the business condition set for search.
