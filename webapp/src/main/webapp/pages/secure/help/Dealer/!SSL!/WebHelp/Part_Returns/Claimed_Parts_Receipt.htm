Claimed Parts Receipt

When the Dealer submits a claim and ships the parts to the OEM, the part inspector either accepts or rejects the parts. If the Part Inspector rejects the parts, and the processor denies the claim, the claim  moves to the dealer's Rejected Parts inbox. After the dealer requests the parts back, the claim moves to Part Shipper. When the Part shipper generates the shipment and prints the shipment tag, the dealer receives the shipped parts details in his Claimed Parts Receipt inbox.

To view the shipped parts details,

  • Click on the Claimed Parts Receipt link under the Action Folders column on your Home page.

  • Click on the Part Returns tab on the left-hand pane. The tab opens to display the folders under it. Click on the Claimed Parts Receipt folder.

Either of the above actions take you to the Claimed Parts Receipt page. On this page, you can see all the parts shipped by the part shipper with their Shipment Number, Warehouse Name, Carrier, Tracking Number and Shipment Date.  Double click on a part to display its details. The details page is read only and only for history purpose. It displays the following sections:

  • Claim Details

  • Part Return Details

  • Shipment Information
