Field Product Improvement SEARCH

To search for a Field Product Improvement claim,

  • Click on PreDefined Search under Search Folders under the Field Product Improvement tab on the left pane.

  • Click on PreDefined Search (Field Product Improvement) under the Common Actions on the Home page.

Either of the above actions will take you to Field Product Improvement Search page. Enter the following details:

  • Class: Use the drop-down field to select the kind of campaign.

  • Dealer Number: Enter the dealer number.

  • Serial Number: Enter the serial number of the item.

  • Field Product Improvement Code: Enter a unique code to identify this campaign.

  • Field Product Improvement Status: Select the Field Product Improvement status: Pending or Complete.

  • Field Product Improvement Ageing: Enter the date range in the From and To field.

Once you set the search parameter, perform one of the following actions:

  • If you wish to save this search, then check the Save Search check-box.

  • If you want to clear the options selected by you, click on the Reset button.

  • If you want to search for inventory items based on the condition you just created, click on the Search button. The page refreshes to show the search results in a tabular format.
