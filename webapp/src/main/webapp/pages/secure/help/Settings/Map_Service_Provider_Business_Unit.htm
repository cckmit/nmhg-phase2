MAP SERVICE PROVIDER BUSINESS UNIT

There are different business units. Each service provider is assigned to a business unit.

 

To assign a business unit to a service provider,

  • Click on Map Service Provider Business Unit link under the Settings tab on the left pane.

  • Enter the Dealer Number.

  • Click Search.

  • Select the business unit from the available list of business units under the Available Business Units column. The Available Business Units lists all the business units available for that dealer.

  • Click Update to save the changes.
