END CUSTOMER MANAGEMENT

The Warranty Management system gives you the option to merge the two or more existing customers into one.

 

Perform the below steps to merge the existing customers:

  • Click on End Customer Management tab under the Settings tab on the left-hand pane. This will reveal the sub folder Merge End Customer. Click on Merge End Customer. You will see a list of companies with their details under the Search Customer section.

  • Select the companies to be merged.

  • Click on Add to Merge List.

  • Select the radio button against the company to which you want to merge the other companies.

  • Click Confirm Merge. A new page will open where you can edit the information for the customer to which you are merging the existing customers.

  • Click Save.

Now, both the existing and the new customer will point to the new customer's address.
