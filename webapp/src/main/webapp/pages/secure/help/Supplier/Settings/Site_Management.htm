SITE MANAGEMENT

Certain parts marked for return to the supplier must be returned to specific locations. These locations are referred to as supplier site. The Warranty Management application allows you to view, create and maintain supplier site in an easy and efficient manner.

 

To view/create a supplier site,

  • Click on Site Management tab under the Settings tab on the left-hand pane. You can view all the supplier locations along with their address.

  • Click on the Create new Site button in the listing toolbar to create a new supplier site and enter the supplier location details on this page.

  • Click Save to save the details entered by you. A success message will be shown confirming your action.

You can now select this new supplier location from the drop-down while maintaining supplier contracts.
