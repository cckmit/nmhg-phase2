REOPENED

The Warranty Management system allows you to reopen a closed recovery claim by querying for such claims. You can reopen any fully accepted and closed, partially accepted and closed and denied and closed recovery claims.

 

To reopen a recovery claim,

  • Search for the closed recovery claim using the search claim option. Click here to know more about searching for recovery claims.

  • In the search result page, open the claim which you want to reopen, in the detail page.

  • At the bottom of the claim detail page you can see the Reopen button. Click on this button.

  • The recovery claim will be reopened and moved to your Reopened Claims folder.

To view all reopened claims, click on the Supplier Recovery tab on the left-hand pane. The tab will open to reveal the folders under it. Click on the Reopened Claims link under the Parts Claimed folder. On this page you can see the Reopened table that lists all closed recovery claims reopened by you. For further information on navigating through the table, see Lists.

 

You can see the following details for each claim in the Reopened table:

  • Recovery Claim Number: Reference number of the recovery claim.

  • Product: Name of the product on which the recovery claim is filed.

  • Model: Name of the model on which the recovery claim is filed.

  • Causal Part: Part serial number or part number of the part which caused the failure in the equipment.

  • Fault Location: Failure location for this claim.

  • Status: The current status of the claim.

You can view the details of any claim by clicking on it. Double clicking on a claim will open it in a new page, <Recovery claim number>. The page for a recovery claim in this folder, are divided into various sections for ease of navigation: Claim Details, Equipment Details, Failure Information, Service Information, Components Replaced, Comments, Manage Documents, Recovery Cost Details, Recovery Claim History, Components Covered, Part Return History and Recovery Comments and Decision. The fields under some sections on this page may be hidden. To view the fields, click on the section header. This will expand the section showing all fields under it. Likewise, clicking on the section header of an expanded section collapses it hiding all fields under it.

 

You can add/edit any details in this claim and perform any of the following actions:

  • On Hold

  • Cannot Recover

  • Transfer

  • Send to Supplier

  • Not For Recovery

  • Accept

You can see the following action buttons on this page:

  • Cancel: Click on this button if you want to close this page without saving any details entered by you for this claim.

  • Submit: Click on this button to submit the claim. Depending upon your selection, the claim will move to respective folder.

A success message will be shown confirming your action.
