InTRODUCTION

A supplier is a person representing the manufacturer who supplies parts to the OEM. Some of the parts sourced by the supplier are covered by a contract. The terms and conditions of the contract are as decided upon and mutually agreed between the supplier and the OEM.

 

When a part sourced by you is the causal part of a claim filed by a service provider, and if the part is covered by a contract, then as per the contract you are expected to reimburse the claim amount to the OEM. As a supplier of parts to the OEM, the Warranty Management system allows you to manage and maintain supply recovery of parts manufactured by you.

 

The Warranty Management application allows you to:

  • Receive and request parts associated with the recovery claims sent to you

  • Review parts and make decisions on accepting or rejecting the recovery claims sent by the OEM

  • Track all parts sent for recovery till the claim is closed

  • Search for parts sent to you for recovery

The Warranty Management application thus allows you to manage supply recovery in an easy and efficient manner.
