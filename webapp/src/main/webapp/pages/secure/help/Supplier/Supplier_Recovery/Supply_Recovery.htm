supplIER Recovery

The Supplier Recovery tab on your left-hand pane lists all folders that assist you in managing all causal parts sent to you for recovery. Depending on the action taken by you in each of the folders, the parts are moved to other folders and then finally removed from your inboxes when the recovery claim associated with the part is closed.

 

The following folders are listed under the Supplier Recovery tab:

  • New: A warranty claim comes in this inbox when it becomes a supplier recovery claim. Any recovery claim sent to the supplier by the supplier recovery processor (SRP) for his action is also added to this supplier inbox. The supplier can choose to make any one of the following decisions on a claim on the New inbox.

  • On Hold

  • Cannot Recover

  • Transfer

  • Send to Supplier

  • Not for Recovery

  • Accept

  • Accepted: If the processor accepts a claim, then the claim comes into this inbox. Clicking on a claim present in this inbox displays all the claim details which are not editable.

  • Disputed: A warranty claim comes in this inbox when the SRA or processor dispute a claim.

  • Confirm Part Returns: When a part is to be received from OEM and the supplier has selected Physical shipment is needed for this part, then it comes to this inbox. Here, the supplier can select receive or not receive option for the part.

  • Reopened Claims: The Warranty Management system allows you to reopen a closed recovery claim by querying for such claims. For further information, click here.

  • Confirm Dealer Part Returns: When processor selects the return location for the part as supplier, then the supplier needs to collect the part from dealer from this inbox.  Here, the supplier can select receive or not receive option for the part.
